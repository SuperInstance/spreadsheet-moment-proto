🔬 **Deep Dive: Our Health Prediction Systems Research (P31)**

One of our standout papers introduces a multi-dimensional health scoring framework for distributed AI systems. Here's what makes it special:

**The Problem:**
Traditional monitoring is reactive—we respond to failures after they occur. But system failures rarely happen instantly; they follow predictable degradation patterns.

**Our Solution:**
A health prediction framework that:
• Aggregates 7 metrics into unified health score
• Learns degradation patterns per component
• Predicts time-to-failure with uncertainty
• Triggers proactive interventions

**Results:**
• 87% correlation with actual failures (p<0.001)
• Prevents 83% of potential failures
• Only 12% false alarm rate
• 47% improvement over single-metric baselines

**Impact:**
This transforms reactive maintenance into proactive intervention, enabling self-healing AI infrastructure.
