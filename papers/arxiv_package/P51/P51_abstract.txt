We introduce mask-locked type systems, where neural network type constraints are physically enforced through metal-layer weight encoding during chip manufacturing. By committing types to silicon at manufacture time, we achieve: (1) zero runtime type checking overhead, (2) guaranteed type safety through physical impossibility of violations, (3) 50× energy efficiency vs. software type checking.
