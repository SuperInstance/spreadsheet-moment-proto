We translate biological dendritic spine geometry (50-200nm neck diameter) into silicon power isolation structures, achieving 3.2× thermal isolation between active domains in 3D-ICs. Mushroom spine ROM (stable weights) and thin spine MRAM (adaptive weights) inform mixed-geometry thermal design.
