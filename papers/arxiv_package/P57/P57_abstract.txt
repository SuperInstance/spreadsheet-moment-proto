We present 3D Stacked Memory for Neural Inference, a vertical integration architecture that co-locates ternary memory arrays with compute logic in a unified 3D-IC stack. By stacking 4 DRAM layers directly atop 1 compute layer with high-density TSVs (10μm pitch, 5μm diameter), we achieve 1.2 TB/s effective bandwidth—8.3× higher than traditional 2D chips with 12× lower energy per access.
