## Core Community Values

### 1. Inclusivity
We welcome everyone regardless of background, experience level, or identity. Diverse perspectives make our community stronger.

### 2. Collaboration
We believe in the power of collaboration over competition. Great ideas come from working together.

### 3. Learning
We're all here to learn - from experts to beginners. Asking questions is encouraged, and teaching others is valued.

### 4. Transparency
We make decisions openly and share our reasoning. Community input shapes our direction.

### 5. Excellence
We strive for quality in everything we do - code, documentation, design, and community interaction.
