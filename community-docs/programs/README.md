# Community Programs

Spreadsheet Moment offers structured programs to help you get more involved, develop skills, and make a bigger impact. Whether you want to represent the community, mentor others, or collaborate on research, there's a program for you.

## Available Programs

### 🌟 Ambassador Program
**Represent Spreadsheet Moment** and help grow our community globally.

- [Learn More](AMBASSADOR.md)
- **Best for**: Active community members who want to represent the project
- **Time Commitment**: 5-10 hours/week
- **Benefits**: Exclusive swag, direct access to maintainers, leadership experience
- **Requirements**: 3+ months active participation, community values alignment

### 🏆 Contributor Recognition
**Get recognized** for your contributions with badges, tiers, and rewards.

- [Learn More](RECOGNITION.md)
- **Best for**: All contributors
- **Recognition**: Automatic based on contributions
- **Benefits**: Badges, physical rewards, career benefits
- **Tracking**: GitHub contributions, forum posts, Discord activity

### 🧪 Beta Testing Program
**Test early features** and shape the product before release.

- [Learn More](BETA_TESTING.md)
- **Best for**: Users who love trying new things
- **Time Commitment**: Variable, based on release cycle
- **Benefits**: Early access to features, direct impact on product, exclusive badge
- **Requirements**: Signed NDA (for unreleased features), testing environment

### 🎤 Speaker Program
**Share your knowledge** at conferences, meetups, and webinars.

- [Learn More](SPEAKERS.md)
- **Best for**: Knowledgeable members who enjoy presenting
- **Time Commitment**: 2-5 hours per presentation
- **Benefits**: Speaker coaching, slide templates, conference support, recognition
- **Requirements**: Expertise in specific area, good presentation skills

### 🔬 Research Collaboration
**Collaborate on academic research** and publish papers.

- [Learn More](RESEARCH.md)
- **Best for**: Academics, researchers, data scientists
- **Time Commitment**: 10+ hours/week during research periods
- **Benefits**: Co-authorship on papers, academic recognition, research funding
- **Requirements**: Research background, IRB approval (if needed), time commitment

### 🤝 Partnership Opportunities
**Formal partnerships** with organizations, companies, and institutions.

- [Learn More](PARTNERSHIPS.md)
- **Best for**: Organizations and representatives
- **Time Commitment**: Variable, based on partnership
- **Benefits**: Co-marketing, technical support, early access, integration support
- **Requirements**: Organizational alignment, mutual benefit, legal agreements

## How to Get Involved

### Step 1: Explore Programs
Read about each program to understand:
- What's involved
- Time commitment
- Requirements
- Benefits
- Application process

### Step 2: Assess Your Fit
Consider:
- **Your interests** - What excites you?
- **Your skills** - What can you contribute?
- **Your availability** - How much time do you have?
- **Your goals** - What do you want to get out of it?

### Step 3: Apply or Participate
Each program has different entry points:
- **Automatic** - Contributor Recognition, some Beta Testing
- **Application** - Ambassador, Speaker, Research
- **Invitation** - Some Partnership opportunities
- **Open** - Anyone can participate in some aspects

### Step 4: Onboarding
If accepted to a program:
- Complete program-specific onboarding
- Meet your program coordinator
- Join program-specific channels
- Set goals and expectations

## FAQ

### Can I participate in multiple programs?
Yes! Many community members participate in multiple programs. Just ensure you have the time to commit to each.

### Are programs paid?
Most programs are voluntary. Some research collaborations may offer stipends or funding. Partnerships may involve financial arrangements.

### What if I don't have much time?
Start with Contributor Recognition (automatic) or Beta Testing (flexible). You can increase involvement as time permits.

### Are programs open internationally?
Yes! All our programs are global. We encourage participation from all time zones and regions.

### How are participants selected?
Each program has its own selection process. Most involve:
- Application form
- Review by coordinators
- Interview (for some programs)
- Response within 1-2 weeks
