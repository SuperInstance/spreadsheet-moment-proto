# Community Guidelines

These guidelines outline our shared values and rules for participating in the Spreadsheet Moment community. By joining our community, you agree to abide by these guidelines.

## Our Core Values

### 1. Respect & Inclusivity 🤝
- Treat everyone with respect, regardless of background, experience level, or identity
- Use inclusive language that welcomes all community members
- Respect different perspectives and experiences
- Celebrate diversity in all its forms

### 2. Collaboration Over Competition 🤲
- Work together rather than compete
- Share knowledge freely
- Credit others for their contributions
- Build on each other's ideas

### 3. Constructive Feedback 📝
- Provide feedback that is specific, actionable, and kind
- Focus on what can be improved, not who is wrong
- Assume good intentions from others
- Accept feedback gracefully

### 4. Learning & Growth 📚
- Embrace mistakes as learning opportunities
- Ask questions freely - there are no stupid questions
- Teach others what you know
- Stay curious and open-minded

### 5. Transparency & Honesty 💎
- Be open about your contributions and interests
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Share your reasoning and process
- Admit when you don't know something

## Community Rules

### Communication Guidelines

#### DO:
- ✅ Be kind and respectful
- ✅ Listen before responding
- ✅ Assume good intentions
- ✅ Use clear, specific language
- ✅ Stay on topic in focused channels
- ✅ Respect channel purposes
- ✅ Give others time to respond
- ✅ Use appropriate channels for different topics

#### DON'T:
- ❌ Use derogatory language or slurs
- ❌ Make personal attacks or insults
- ❌ Engage in harassment or bullying
- ❌ Spam or self-promote excessively
- ❌ Post off-topic in focused channels
- ❌ Share others' private information
- ❌ Engage in flame wars or trolling
- ❌ Discriminate based on identity, background, or beliefs

### Contribution Guidelines

#### DO:
- ✅ Follow project contribution guidelines
- ✅ Write clear commit messages
- ✅ Test your changes before submitting
- ✅ Document your work
- ✅ Respond to review feedback
- ✅ Be patient with the review process
- ✅ Credit collaborators
- ✅ Start with small contributions

#### DON'T:
- ❌ Submit untested code
- ❌ Ignore review feedback
- ❌ Make large, uncommented changes
- ❌ Claim others' work as your own
- ❌ Demand immediate reviews
- ❌ Skip documentation
- ❌ Work on paid features without coordination

### Privacy & Security Guidelines

#### DO:
- ✅ Protect your personal information
- ✅ Use strong, unique passwords
- ✅ Enable two-factor authentication
- ✅ Report security vulnerabilities privately
- ✅ Respect others' privacy
- ✅ Follow data protection regulations

#### DON'T:
- ❌ Share others' private information
- ❌ Post private conversations publicly
- ❌ Attempt to access others' accounts
- ❌ Share security vulnerabilities publicly
- ❌ Violate data protection laws

### Intellectual Property Guidelines

#### DO:
- ✅ Respect copyright and licenses
- ✅ Credit original creators
- ✅ Use appropriate licenses for your work
- ✅ Understand open source licensing
- ✅ Ask permission when unsure
- ✅ Follow trademark guidelines

#### DON'T:
- ❌ Plagiarize others' work
- ❌ Violate license terms
- ❌ Claim trademarks without permission
- ❌ Use proprietary code inappropriately
- ❌ Misrepresent attribution

## Conflict Resolution

### If You Experience Conflict
1. **Take a break** - Step away and cool down
2. **Assume good faith** - Try to understand the other perspective
3. **Communicate directly** - Try to resolve the issue privately
4. **Seek mediation** - Ask a moderator for help if needed
5. **Accept decisions** - Respect moderator decisions

### Moderation Process
1. **Warning** - First offense usually gets a warning
2. **Temporary Suspension** - Repeated or serious offenses
3. **Permanent Ban** - Severe violations or repeated offenses

### Appeals Process
If you believe a moderation action is unfair:
1. Review the specific guideline that was violated
2. Write a calm, detailed explanation to trust@spreadsheetmoment.com
3. Include evidence and context
4. Wait for a response from the steering committee

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience a guideline violation:
1. **Document the incident** - Save screenshots/links
2. **Report privately** - Email trust@spreadsheetmoment.com
3. **DM a moderator** - For urgent issues on Discord
4. **Do not engage** - Let moderators handle it

### Consequences
Consequences vary based on severity:
- **Minor**: Reminder or warning
- **Moderate**: Temporary suspension (24 hours to 7 days)
- **Severe**: Permanent ban

### Zero-Tolerance Violations
These result in immediate, permanent bans:
- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Threats of violence or harm
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Sexual harassment or misconduct
- Illegal activities
- Deliberate disruption of community operations

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "I made a mistake. What should I do?"
- Own it, apologize sincerely, and make it right
- Learn from it and move forward
- We all make mistakes - it's how we grow

### "Someone is being rude to me. What should I do?"
- Don't engage or respond
- Document the interaction
- Report to moderators
- Take care of yourself

### "I disagree with a moderation decision."
- Review the guidelines and context
- Calmly explain your perspective
- Accept that moderators make hard calls
- Focus on moving forward constructively

### "Can I promote my own project?"
- Yes, if it's relevant and not excessive
- Be transparent about your involvement
- Focus on value to the community
- Follow self-promotion guidelines

### "What if I'm not sure if something is okay?"
- Ask! We're happy to clarify
- Email community@spreadsheetmoment.com
- Ask in #meta on Discord
- Better to ask than to guess
