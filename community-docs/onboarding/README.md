# Welcome to Spreadsheet Moment! 🎉

Congratulations on joining the Spreadsheet Moment community! We're thrilled to have you. Whether you're here to learn, contribute, collaborate, or just hang out, you've found an amazing community of spreadsheet enthusiasts, developers, designers, and innovators.

## What is Spreadsheet Moment?

Spreadsheet Moment is a next-generation collaborative spreadsheet platform that combines the power of traditional spreadsheets with modern collaboration features, real-time syncing, AI assistance, and a vibrant community ecosystem.

### Our Mission
> To empower everyone to organize data, collaborate seamlessly, and solve problems through spreadsheets - regardless of technical expertise.

### What Makes Us Different
- **Real-time Collaboration** - Work together with your team in real-time
- **Modern UX** - Beautiful, intuitive interface
- **AI-Powered** - Smart assistance and automation
- **Community-Driven** - Built by the community, for the community
- **Open Source** - Transparent development and contributions

## Community Roles

### New Member (0-30 days)
You're just getting started! Focus on:
- Setting up your profile
- Reading guidelines
- Making your first contributions
- Connecting with others

### Active Member (30+ days)
You're a regular participant! Keep up:
- Helping newcomers
- Contributing regularly
- Providing feedback
- Attending events

You've made substantive contributions! You can:
- Submit pull requests
- Review documentation
- Mentor newcomers
- Lead initiatives

- Reviewing pull requests

- Content creation

## Getting Help

### I'm Stuck. Where Do I Go?
1. **Search first** - Check our [FAQ](../support/FAQ.md) and [knowledge base](../support/KNOWLEDGE_BASE.md)
2. **Ask on Discord** - Join the appropriate channel
3. **Post in Forum** - For detailed questions
4. **Attend Office Hours** - Live Q&A with maintainers

### I Want to Contribute but Don't Know How
1. Read [First Contribution Guide](../contributing/FIRST_CONTRIBUTION.md)
2. Check "good first issue" on GitHub
3. Join the `#contributor-help` Discord channel
4. Attend a contributor workshop

### I'm Experiencing Harassment
This is not okay. Please:
1. Report immediately to trust@spreadsheetmoment.com
2. DM a moderator on Discord
3. Do not engage with the harasser
4. Read our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for procedures

## Common First-Time Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

### ❌ Don't: Post the same question in multiple places
✅ Do: Choose the most appropriate channel and wait for responses

### ❌ Don't: DM maintainers directly for help
✅ Do: Post in public channels where others can learn too

### ❌ Don't: Expect instant responses
✅ Do: Be patient - we're a global community across time zones

### ❌ Don't: Contribute without reading guidelines
✅ Do: Always read contribution guidelines first

### ❌ Don't: Be afraid to ask "stupid" questions
✅ Do: Ask away - there are no stupid questions!
