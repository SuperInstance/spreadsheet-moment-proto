# Communication Channels

Welcome to our communication ecosystem! We use various platforms for different purposes. Here's your guide to finding the right channel for your needs.

## Primary Channels

### 1. Discord Server 💬
**Best for**: Real-time chat, quick questions, social interaction

**Join**: [discord.gg/spreadsheetmoment](https://discord.gg/spreadsheetmoment)

#### Key Channels
- **#announcements** - Official updates (read-only)
- **#introductions** - Say hello when you join!
- **#general** - General chat and community building
- **#help** - Get help with problems
- **#contributor-help** - Questions about contributing
- **#show-and-tell** - Share what you're working on
- **#feature-discussions** - Discuss new features
- **#off-topic** - Casual conversation (respectful)

#### Working Group Channels
- **#dev** - Development discussions
- **#design** - UX/UI design
- **#docs** - Documentation improvements
- **#community** - Community building and events
- **#translation** - Translation efforts

#### Language Channels
- **#español** - Spanish speakers
- **#中文** - Chinese speakers
- **#français** - French speakers
- **#deutsch** - German speakers
- **#日本語** - Japanese speakers
- **#português** - Portuguese speakers
- **#other-languages** - Request new language channels

#### Discord Etiquette
- Use appropriate channels for topics
- Don't DM maintainers unless they invite it
- Be patient - responses may take time
- Use threads for long conversations
- Keep messages clear and concise
- Use appropriate emojis and reactions

## Communication Norms

### Response Time Expectations

#### Discord
- **Expected**: Minutes to hours
- **Acceptable**: Up to 24 hours
- **After 24h**: Polite bump is okay

#### GitHub
- **Expected**: 1-3 business days
- **Acceptable**: Up to 1 week
- **After 1 week**: Polite bump is okay

#### Forum
- **Expected**: 1-3 days
- **Acceptable**: Up to 1 week
- **After 1 week**: Polite bump is okay

#### Email
- **Expected**: 2-3 business days
- **Acceptable**: Up to 1 week
- **After 1 week**: Polite follow-up is okay

### Time Zones
We're a global community across all time zones. Please:
- Be patient with responses
- Specify your time zone if scheduling
- Use asynchronous communication when possible
- Consider others' working hours

### Language
Primary language is English, but we welcome all languages:
- Use English for official channels
- Use language-specific channels for other languages
- Use translation tools when needed
- Be patient with language barriers

## Best Practices

### Getting Help Quickly
1. **Search first** - Check if your question was already answered
2. **Choose the right channel** - Use the guide above
3. **Be specific** - Provide context, details, and what you've tried
4. **Use code blocks** - Format code properly
5. **Share links** - Link to relevant issues, docs, or posts
6. **Follow up** - Let us know if the solution worked

### Effective Communication
- **Be clear** - State your question or issue clearly
- **Be concise** - Don't ramble, but include necessary details
- **Be kind** - Assume good intentions
- **Be patient** - Wait for responses
- **Be grateful** - Thank those who help you

### Avoiding Common Mistakes
- ❌ Posting the same question in multiple places
- ❌ DMing maintainers directly
- ❌ Demanding immediate responses
- ❌ Being rude or impatient
- ❌ Not providing enough context
- ❌ Not searching before asking
