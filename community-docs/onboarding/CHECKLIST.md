# Getting Started Checklist

Welcome to Spreadsheet Moment! This checklist will guide you through your first week in the community. Complete these items to get fully integrated and set up for success.

## Day 1: Account Setup 🎯

### Profile Setup
- [ ] **Create your account** on spreadsheetmoment.com
- [ ] **Complete your profile** with:
  - [ ] Display name
  - [ ] Profile picture (avatar)
  - [ ] Short bio (50-150 characters)
  - [ ] Location/timezone
  - [ ] Areas of interest (coding, design, business, etc.)
  - [ ] Skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- [ ] **Verify your email address**
- [ ] **Enable two-factor authentication** (2FA)
- [ ] **Set notification preferences**

### Platform Exploration
- [ ] **Take the platform tour** (if available)
- [ ] **Create your first spreadsheet** (even if blank!)
- [ ] **Explore the template gallery**
- [ ] **Bookmark your favorite templates**

### Community Introduction
- [ ] **Join our Discord server** using the invite link
- [ ] **Post an introduction** in `#introductions`:
  ```
  Hi everyone! 👋

  I'm [Name] from [Location]. I'm excited to join the Spreadsheet Moment community!

  A bit about me:
  - I'm interested in [interests]
  - I'm here to [learn/contribute/collaborate]
  - Fun fact: [optional]

  Looking forward to getting to know you all!
  ```

### Time Estimate: 30-45 minutes

---

## Day 3: First Explorations 🔍

### Platform Features
- [ ] **Try basic spreadsheet operations**:
  - [ ] Create a new spreadsheet
  - [ ] Enter data
  - [ ] Use basic formulas
  - [ ] Format cells
  - [ ] Save your work
- [ ] **Explore collaboration features**:
  - [ ] Share a spreadsheet
  - [ ] Try commenting
  - [ ] Test version history

### Community Engagement
- [ ] **Introduce yourself in the forum** (create a new post)
- [ ] **Comment on 3 existing posts** in the forum or Discord
- [ ] **React to 5 messages** in Discord
- [ ] **Like or upvote helpful content**

### Learning Resources
- [ ] **Read 2 blog posts** from our blog
- [ ] **Watch 1 video tutorial**
- [ ] **Explore 3 community templates**

### Time Estimate: 30-45 minutes

---

## Day 4: Finding Your Niche 🎨

### Identify Your Interests
Choose areas you want to focus on:
- [ ] **Coding/Development**
- [ ] **Design/UX**
- [ ] **Data Analysis**
- [ ] **Business/Productivity**
- [ ] **Education/Teaching**
- [ ] **Community Building**
- [ ] **Documentation/Writing**
- [ ] **Translation**

### Connect with Like-Minded Members
- [ ] **Find channels for your interests** on Discord
- [ ] **Introduce yourself** in those channels
- [ ] **Follow 5 community members** with similar interests
- [ ] **Join relevant working groups** (if available)

### Set Personal Goals
Write down your goals for the next month:
- [ ] My primary goal: ________________
- [ ] My secondary goal: ________________

- [ ] Contributions I want to make: ________________

### Time Estimate: 20-30 minutes

## Day 5: First Contribution 🛠️

### Choose Your First Contribution
Pick ONE to start:
- [ ] **Help another user** - Answer a question in the forum or Discord
- [ ] **Report a bug** - Find and document a bug
- [ ] **Suggest a feature** - Propose an improvement
- [ ] **Improve documentation** - Fix a typo or clarify text
- [ ] **Share a template** - Share a useful spreadsheet
- [ ] **Give feedback** - Provide constructive feedback on something

### Complete Your Contribution
- [ ] **Follow contribution guidelines** for your chosen type
- [ ] **Submit your contribution** (PR, issue, forum post, etc.)
- [ ] **Track your contribution** using the tracking system
- [ ] **Celebrate your first contribution!** 🎉

### Reflect
- [ ] **What went well?** ________________
- [ ] **What could be improved?** ________________
- [ ] **What will I do differently next time?** ________________

### Time Estimate: 30-60 minutes
