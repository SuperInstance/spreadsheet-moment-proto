## Critical Decisions Made

1. **Model**: iFairy (complex weights) or BitNet (ternary)
2. **Memory**: On-chip SRAM with sliding window KV cache
3. **Process**: 28nm TSMC
4. **Architecture**: 32×32 systolic array of RAUs
