## Why It Works

**Mask-locked architecture** encodes neural weights in silicon metal layers, eliminating:
- Memory bandwidth bottlenecks
- Weight fetch energy (95% reduction)
- Software drivers entirely

**iFairy complex weights** replace multiplication with simple permutation—making 28nm silicon perform like 7nm for LLM inference.
