## The Problem

Running AI at the edge is broken. Developers choose between $250 Jetson boards that consume 15W and require days of CUDA setup, or $90 Hailo accelerators that deliver 5 tokens/second—slower than a CPU. There's no affordable, plug-and-play solution for local LLM inference. Meanwhile, 70 million Raspberry Pi owners, 500,000 robotics developers, and countless IoT manufacturers need intelligence that doesn't require a cloud connection or a PhD in deployment.

## The Solution

SuperInstance is a physical AI cartridge. We embed neural network weights directly into silicon metal layers—mask-locked at manufacture—creating the first truly plug-and-play AI hardware. No drivers. No software stack. No memory bottleneck. Insert the cartridge, get intelligence.

Each cartridge is a complete inference engine: a 2B parameter model encoded in hardware, delivering 80-150 tokens/second at 2-3 watts. That's 5x faster than Hailo at 1/3 the power. The cartridge architecture means models are swappable without software—different cartridges for different tasks, like loading a game into a console.
