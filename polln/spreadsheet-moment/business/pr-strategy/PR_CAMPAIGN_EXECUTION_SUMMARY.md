## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**Risk 1: Limited Media Interest**
- Mitigation: Multiple story angles for different beats
- Mitigation: Embargoed exclusives for top outlets
- Mitigation: Data-driven pitches with customer stories

**Risk 2: Negative Coverage**
- Mitigation: Crisis communication plan prepared
- Mitigation: Rapid response team assembled
- Mitigation: Transparency and authenticity in messaging

**Risk 3: Competitor Announcements**
- Mitigation: Unique differentiators (AI agents, 10K users, accessibility)
- Mitigation: Focus on innovation, not features
- Mitigation: Open source as trust signal

**Risk 4: Technical Issues at Launch**
- Mitigation: Extensive pre-launch testing
- Mitigation: Prepared statements for service issues
- Mitigation: Customer support ready for influx

**Risk 5: Executive Availability**
- Mitigation: Multiple spokespeople trained
- Mitigation: Block scheduling for interviews
- Mitigation: Backup spokespeople identified
