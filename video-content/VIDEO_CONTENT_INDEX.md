## Script Templates

### Standard Script Format

All scripts follow this structure:

```markdown
# Video Title
**Duration:** X:XX minutes
**Target Audience:** [Description]
**Level:** Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced

## Scene 1: Title (0:00 - 0:XX)
**Visual:** [Description]
**Audio:** [Voiceover text]
**Screen Capture:** [Actions]

## Production Notes
[Technical requirements, assets needed, etc.]

## SEO Metadata
[Title, description, tags, keywords]
```

### Script Elements

Each script includes:
- **Scene-by-scene breakdowns**
- **Visual direction**
- **Audio/voiceover text**
- **Screen capture instructions**
- **Production notes**
- **SEO metadata**
- **Quality checklist**

## Quality Standards

### Video Quality
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps
- **Bitrate:** 10,000 kbps (VBR, 2-pass)
- **Format:** H.264 MP4

### Audio Quality
- **Sample Rate:** 48kHz
- **Bit Depth:** 24-bit
- **Format:** AAC, 320kbps
- **Voice Level:** -6dB

### Content Quality
- **Technical Accuracy:** 100%
- **Code Examples:** Tested and working
- **Clarity:** Beginner-friendly
- **Pacing:** Appropriate for topic
