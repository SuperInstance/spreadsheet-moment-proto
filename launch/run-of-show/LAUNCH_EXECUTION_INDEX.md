## Risk Management

### Critical Risks & Mitigations

**Technical:**
- Infrastructure failure under load → Load testing, auto-scaling, CDN
- Critical bugs during launch → Beta testing, rapid response team

**Market:**
- Low launch day turnout → Pre-launch waitlist, influencer partnerships
- Competitor launches same day → Differentiated positioning, response templates

**Execution:**
- Team burnout → Clear roles, on-call rotations, post-launch rest

**Messaging:**
- Value proposition unclear → A/B testing, user feedback, demo-first approach
