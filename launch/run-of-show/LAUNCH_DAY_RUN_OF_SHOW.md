## 5. Crisis Procedures

### 5.1 Crisis Levels

**P0 - Critical (Launch-Threatening):**
- Website down
- Payment processing broken
- Data breach suspected
- Major security flaw

**Response:**
1. Immediate alert in #launch-alerts
2. All hands on deck in voice channel
3. Launch Commander directs response
4. Public statement within 15 minutes
5. Regular updates every 15 minutes

**P1 - High (Impactful):**
- Significant bug affecting many users
- Performance degradation
- Feature not working as advertised

**Response:**
1. Alert in #launch-alerts
2. Relevant team investigates
3. Launch Commander informed
4. Public communication if user-facing
5. Resolution update every 30 minutes

**P2 - Medium (Manageable):**
- Minor bug affecting some users
- Performance not optimal
- Feature not critical

**Response:**
1. Alert in relevant channel
2. Team triages and prioritizes
3. Document for post-launch fix
4. Communicate if user-facing

### 5.2 Crisis Scenarios & Responses

#### Scenario 1: Website Down
**Detection:** Monitoring alert
**Response:**
1. Verify outage (is it us or CDN?)
2. Check status page update
3. Restart services if needed
4. Communicate on social: "We're experiencing issues, investigating"
5. Restore services
6. Postmortem communication

#### Scenario 2: Payment Processing Broken
**Detection:** User reports, monitoring
**Response:**
1. Verify payment gateway status
2. Check ifStripe/PayPal issue
3. Pause signups if critical
4. Communicate: "Payment issues, working on fix"
5. Fix or implement workaround
6. Compensate affected users

#### Scenario 3: Negative Press/Viral Criticism
**Detection:** Social monitoring, press alerts
**Response:**
1. Don't respond defensively
2. Acknowledge valid criticisms
3. Correct factual errors
4. Offer to discuss concerns
5. Focus on improvements
6. Let community defend (authentic)

#### Scenario 4: Competitor Launch Same Day
**Detection:** Market monitoring
**Response:**
1. Don't engage directly
2. Reinforce differentiation
3. Highlight customer success
4. Focus on innovation roadmap
5. Take high road
6. Let community defend

### 5.3 Crisis Communication Templates

**Initial Statement (within 15 min):**
```
We're currently experiencing [issue]. Our team is investigating and working to resolve it as quickly as possible. We'll provide updates here every 15 minutes.

Thank you for your patience.
```

**Update Statement (every 15-30 min):**
```
Update on [issue]: [progress made]. We expect to have this resolved within [timeframe]. Next update at [time].

Current status: [brief status]
```

**Resolution Statement:**
```
[Issue] has been resolved. We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your patience.

As a gesture of goodwill, we're offering [compensation].

Here's what happened: [transparent explanation]
Here's what we're doing to prevent this: [prevention steps]
```
