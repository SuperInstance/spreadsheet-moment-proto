## Crisis Communication Plan

### Negative Press Protocol

**Scenario 1: Critical Article Published**
1. Assess validity of criticism
2. Don't respond defensively
3. Acknowledge valid points
4. Correct factual errors
5. Offer to discuss concerns
6. Focus on improvements
7. Let community defend authentically

**Scenario 2: Factually Incorrect Coverage**
1. Contact journalist privately
2. Provide correct information
3. Request correction/clarification
4. If refused, public correction
5. Document for transparency

**Scenario 3: Competitor Comparison (Negative)**
1. Don't engage directly
2. Reinforce differentiation
3. Highlight customer success
4. Focus on innovation roadmap
5. Take high road
