# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

* **Respect**: Valuing each other's ideas, styles, and viewpoints
* **Inclusivity**: Welcoming and supporting diverse perspectives
* **Collaboration**: Working together to solve problems
* **Empathy**: Understanding and responding to the needs of others
* **Constructive Feedback**: Providing and receiving feedback gracefully
* **Focus**: Focusing on what is best for the community
* **Growth**: Learning from mistakes and accepting feedback

### Unacceptable Behavior

* **Harassment**: Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention,
  or trolling
* **Exclusion**: Deliberate exclusion, intimidation, or discrimination
* **Disrespect**: Insults, derogatory comments, or personal/political attacks
* **Disruption**: Disrupting discussions, presentations, or events
* **Aggression**: Threatening, intimidating, or harassing behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

### Community Spaces Include

* GitHub repositories (issues, PRs, discussions)
* Official Discord server
* Official social media channels
* Public and private meetings
* Conferences and events

### Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at
conduct@superinstance.ai.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Correction Strategies

**1. Warning**
- Private communication from community leaders
- Clear explanation of the violation
- Opportunity for corrective action

**2. Temporary Suspension**
- Temporary exclusion from community spaces
- Duration based on severity
- Review at end of suspension

**3. Permanent Ban**
- For severe or repeated violations
- No right to appeal in extreme cases
