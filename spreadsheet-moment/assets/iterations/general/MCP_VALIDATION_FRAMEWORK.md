## Phase 2: Multi-Model Validation Plan

### Validation Models Available

#### 1. Groq (FREE - Fast Iteration)
- **Model:** Llama 3 70B
- **Purpose:** Quick feedback, accessibility check
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Use:** Initial validation, quick iterations

#### 2. DeepInfra (Diverse Perspectives)
- **Models:** Llama 3 70B, Qwen 2 72B, Nemo 340B
- **Purpose:** Diverse model perspectives
- **Cost:** Very low ($0.07/MM tokens)
- **Use:** Comprehensive validation

#### 3. DeepSeek (Reasoning Focus)
- **Model:** DeepSeek-Chat
- **Purpose:** Logical consistency, reasoning quality
- **Cost:** Low ($0.14/MM tokens)
- **Use:** Technical accuracy check

#### 4. Alibaba Qwen (Cross-Cultural)
- **Model:** Qwen 2 72B
- **Purpose:** Cross-cultural accessibility
- **Cost:** Low
- **Use:** International audience check

#### 5. Kimi (Accessibility Analysis)
- **Model:** Moonshot v1
- **Purpose:** Deep accessibility analysis
- **Cost:** Moderate
- **Use:** Final accessibility validation

### Validation Questions for Each Model

#### Accessibility Questions
1. Is this document understandable to non-technical audiences?
2. Are all technical terms explained with analogies?
3. Is the tone appropriate for general audiences?
4. Are the examples relatable to everyday life?
5. Would a grandmother understand this?

#### Content Questions
1. Does this document clearly explain the problems?
2. Are the solutions explained in simple terms?
3. Is the connection between problem and solution clear?
4. Are the real-world applications convincing?
5. Is the future vision inspiring but realistic?

#### Structure Questions
1. Is the document well-organized?
2. Does it flow logically from problem to solution?
3. Is the length appropriate for the content?
4. Are the sections balanced?
5. Is the conclusion satisfying?

#### Suggestion Questions
1. What sections need improvement?
2. What analogies work best/worst?
3. What's missing from the document?
4. What would make this more engaging?
5. How could we simplify further?
