## Executive Summary

Spreadsheet Moment employs a diversified revenue model designed to maximize customer lifetime value while maintaining accessibility and fostering growth. Our strategy combines subscription-based recurring revenue with usage-based monetization and professional services to create multiple revenue streams.

### Total Revenue Composition (Year 3 Projection)
- **Subscription Revenue:** 75% ($15.75M ARR)
- **Usage-Based Revenue:** 15% ($3.15M ARR)
- **Professional Services:** 7% ($1.47M ARR)
- **Marketplace Fees:** 3% ($630K ARR)

---

## Primary Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Revenue (75%)

#### Individual Subscriptions
**Target Market:** Freelancers, consultants, students, power users

**Free Tier**
- **Revenue:** $0
- **Purpose:** User acquisition, product-market fit validation
- **Conversion Target:** 5% to paid tiers within 90 days

**Pro Tier - $12/month ($120/year)**
- **Features:** Unlimited spreadsheets, AI agents, API access (10K calls/month)
- **Target:** 40% of paid users
- **ARPU:** $12/month
- **Revenue Model:** Monthly/annual billing (17% discount for annual)

**Projected Revenue (Year 3):**
- Individual Users: 25,000
- Average Revenue: $12/month
- Monthly Revenue: $300,000
- Annual Revenue: $3,600,000

#### Team Subscriptions
**Target Market:** Startups, SMBs, departmental teams

**Team Tier - $49/user/month ($490/user/year)**
- **Features:** Collaboration, team admin, advanced permissions, 100 GB storage
- **Minimum:** 3 users
- **Target:** 50% of paid users
- **ARPU:** $49/month
- **Billing:** Monthly/annual (team-wide billing)

**Projected Revenue (Year 3):**
- Team Users: 20,000
- Average Revenue: $49/month
- Monthly Revenue: $980,000
- Annual Revenue: $11,760,000

#### Enterprise Subscriptions
**Target Market:** Mid-market to enterprise (100+ employees)

**Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing**
- **Starting Point:** $100/user/year
- **Features:** SSO, unlimited storage, dedicated support, SLA, custom integrations
- **Contract Length:** 1-3 years
- **Target:** 10% of paid users
- **ARPU:** $150/month

**Projected Revenue (Year 3):**
- Enterprise Users: 5,000
- Average Revenue: $150/month
- Monthly Revenue: $750,000
- Annual Revenue: $9,000,000

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### 1. Pricing Packaging Optimization
- **Tier Positioning:** Clear value differentiation between tiers
- **Anchor Pricing:** Use Enterprise tier to make Team tier feel valuable
- **Bundling:** Combine complementary features to increase perceived value
- **Decoy Effect:** Strategic pricing to guide tier selection

### 2. Billing Optimization
- **Annual Billing Incentives:** 17% discount for annual commitments
- **Multi-Year Contracts:** Additional 10% discount for 2-3 year commitments
- **Prepayment Options:** 5% discount for 1-year prepayment
- **Payment Methods:** Credit card, ACH, wire transfer

### 3. Expansion Revenue
- **Seat Expansion:** Grow team size within existing customers
- **Tier Upgrades:** Pro → Team → Enterprise progression
- **Feature Add-ons:** Premium features for additional fees
- **Usage Expansion:** Increased API/AI usage over time

### 4. Retention & Churn Reduction
- **Value Realization:** Ensure customers achieve ROI quickly
- **Customer Success:** Proactive support and best practices
- **Community Building:** User groups and knowledge sharing
- **Continuous Innovation:** Regular feature releases

## Revenue Recognition Principles

### 1. Subscription Revenue
- **Timing:** Recognized ratably over subscription period
- **Billing:** Advance billing for monthly/annual subscriptions
- **Revenue Recognition:** ASC 606 compliance

### 2. Usage-Based Revenue
- **Timing:** Recognized as usage occurs
- **Billing:** Monthly arrears billing for overages
- **Measurement:** Metered and invoiced based on actual usage

### 3. Professional Services
- **Timing:** Recognized upon service delivery
- **Billing:** 50% upfront, 50% upon completion
- **Milestone-Based:** Larger projects billed by milestones
