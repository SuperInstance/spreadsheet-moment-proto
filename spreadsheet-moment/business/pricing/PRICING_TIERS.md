## Executive Summary

Spreadsheet Moment employs a four-tier pricing strategy designed to capture value across the customer spectrum while maintaining accessibility for individual users and scaling appropriately for teams and enterprises.

### Pricing Philosophy
1. **Value-Based Pricing:** Price based on customer value realization
2. **Transparent Pricing:** Simple, predictable pricing with no hidden fees
3. **Fair Exchange:** Price commensurate with value delivered
4. **Growth Incentives:** Pricing that encourages adoption and expansion

## Pricing Governance

### Pricing Principles
1. **No Hidden Fees:** Transparent, predictable pricing
2. **Grandfathering:** Never increase prices for existing customers
3. **Price Anchoring:** Use higher tiers to make lower tiers valuable
4. **Value Alignment:** Price increases tied to value increases
5. **Customer Fairness:** Pricing should feel fair and justified

### Pricing Change Process
1. **Proposal:** Data-driven proposal with rationale
2. **Review:** Cross-functional review (Product, Sales, Finance)
3. **Testing:** A/B test with customer segment
4. **Communication:** Clear communication to customers
5. **Implementation:** Phased rollout with monitoring
