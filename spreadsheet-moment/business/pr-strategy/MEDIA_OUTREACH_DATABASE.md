## Outreach Tracking Spreadsheet Template

### Column Headers

**Contact Information:**
- Publication/Outlet
- Journalist Name
- Beat/Focus
- Email Address
- Twitter Handle
- LinkedIn Profile
- Phone Number (if available)

**Pitch Information:**
- Primary Angle
- Secondary Angle
- Pitch Sent Date
- Follow-up Date
- Response Received
- Coverage Result

**Status Tracking:**
- Contact Status (Cold, Warm, Hot)
- Outreach Status (Not Contacted, Contacted, Responded, Coverage Secured)
- Interest Level (Low, Medium, High)
- Next Action Required

**Coverage Metrics:**
- Article URL (if published)
- Article Type (News, Feature, Brief)
- Word Count
- Impressions/Reach
- Social Shares
- Backlinks Generated
- Sentiment (Positive, Neutral, Negative)

**ROI Tracking:**
- Time Invested (hours)
- Cost (if paid placement)
- Sign-ups Attributed
- Demo Requests
- Enterprise Inquiries
- Revenue Impact
