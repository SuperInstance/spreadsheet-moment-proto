## Crisis Communication Plan

### Potential Crisis Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Negative Media Coverage**
- **Trigger:** Critical article from major publication
- **Response Time:** Within 2 hours
- **Actions:**
  1. Assess validity and severity of criticism
  2. Prepare response statement
  3. Offer correction or clarification if factual errors
  4. Provide additional context or data
  5. Offer interview or briefing to address concerns
  6. Monitor response and adjust messaging

**Scenario 2: Technical Outage or Security Issue**
- **Trigger:** Platform downtime or data breach
- **Response Time:** Immediate (within 30 minutes)
- **Actions:**
  1. Issue transparent statement acknowledging issue
  2. Provide regular updates on resolution
  3. Offer customer support resources
  4. Post-incident: Share lessons learned
  5. Demonstrate commitment to improvement

**Scenario 3: Executive Controversy**
- **Trigger:** Negative comments or behavior by executive
- **Response Time:** Within 1 hour
- **Actions:**
  1. Assess severity and potential impact
  2. Issue statement if needed (apology, clarification)
  3. Executive steps back from media temporarily
  4. Other executives available for interviews
  5. Review and reinforce company values

**Scenario 4: Competitive Attack**
- **Trigger:** Competitor launches negative campaign
- **Response Time:** Within 4 hours
- **Actions:**
  1. Monitor competitive messaging
  2. Avoid direct public confrontation
  3. Reinforce own value propositions
  4. Provide customer testimonials
  5. Focus on product differentiation
  6. Let product quality speak

**Scenario 5: Customer Complaint Goes Viral**
- **Trigger:** Customer complaint gains media attention
- **Response Time:** Within 2 hours
- **Actions:**
  1. Acknowledge customer concern publicly
  2. Offer to resolve issue directly
  3. Share resolution publicly when appropriate
  4. Demonstrate commitment to customer satisfaction
  5. Use as opportunity to show responsiveness
