## Influencer Partnership Strategy

### Partnership Philosophy

**Authentic First:** We don't want fake endorsements. We want authentic feedback from creators we respect. If an influencer tries Spreadsheet Moment and hates it, no pressure to promote.

**Value Exchange:** Influencers bring access and trust. We bring product innovation and fair compensation. It's a partnership, not a transaction.

**Long-Term Relationships:** We're building lasting relationships with creators, not one-off promotions. The best partnerships grow and evolve over time.

**Creative Freedom:** Influencers know their audience best. We provide guidance and assets, but they control the creative.

## Influencer Partnership Process

### Phase 1: Identification & Research (Week 1-2)

**Identify Target Influencers:**
- Research creators in productivity, tech, accessibility niches
- Analyze audience demographics and engagement
- Assess content quality and authenticity
- Check for competitor partnerships
- Verify audience match with Spreadsheet Moment

**Create Influencer Profiles:**
- Basic info (name, platforms, follower counts)
- Content analysis (topics, style, frequency)
- Audience demographics (age, location, interests)
- Engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)
- Partnership history (brands they've worked with)
- Contact information (email, agency, manager)

**Prioritize Targets:**
- Tier 1: Must-have mega-influencers (10 targets)
- Tier 2: High-value macro-influencers (30 targets)
- Tier 3: Niche micro-influencers (60+ targets)
- Tier 4: Accessibility advocates (20+ targets)

### Phase 2: Outreach & Negotiation (Week 3-6)

**Initial Outreach:**
- Personalized emails referencing their content
- Clear value proposition for their audience
- Multiple partnership options (no pressure)
- Offer early access before commitment
- Response within 24-48 hours

**Follow-up Sequence:**
- Day 3-5: First follow-up
- Day 7-10: Second follow-up with new angle
- Day 14: Final follow-up or close the loop

**Negotiation:**
- Discuss deliverables and timeline
- Agree on compensation (sponsored, affiliate, ambassador)
- Clarify creative freedom and approval process
- Define usage rights and exclusivity terms
- Contract and agreement signing

### Phase 3: Onboarding & Content Creation (Week 7-12)

**Influencer Onboarding:**
- Provide product access and training
- Share key messages and differentiators
- Provide assets (logos, screenshots, b-roll)
- Offer access to product team for questions
- Set up tracking links and dashboards

**Content Creation:**
- Influencer creates content in their style
- Check-ins for support and feedback
- Optional: review draft for factual accuracy
- Final approval (or consultation depending on tier)
- Schedule publication date

**Content Distribution:**
- Influencer publishes on their channels
- Spreadsheet Moment amplifies across channels
- Cross-promotion on social media
- Featured in newsletters and blog
- Paid promotion for top-performing content

### Phase 4: Performance Tracking (Ongoing)

**Metrics to Track:**
- Views, reach, impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate to website
- Sign-ups attributed to influencer
- Demo requests generated
- Pipeline and revenue impact

**ROI Calculation:**
- Total spend (sponsored fees + retainer)
- Total sign-ups attributed
- Cost per acquisition
- Customer lifetime value
- Return on investment ratio

**Performance Reporting:**
- Weekly dashboard for top partners
- Monthly summary for all partners
- Quarterly review and optimization
- Annual partnership evaluation

### Phase 5: Relationship Management (Ongoing)

**Ongoing Communication:**
- Regular check-ins (not just when asking for something)
- Share company updates and product news
- Ask for feedback on product and roadmap
- Invite to company events and launches
- Celebrate their successes and milestones

**Partnership Evolution:**
- Start with sponsored content or affiliate
- Evaluate performance and fit
- Evolve to ambassador relationship if mutual value
- Consider co-branded products or features
- Long-term equity or advisory roles for top partners

**Community Building:**
- Create influencer community (Slack/Discord)
- Facilitate connections between influencers
- Host virtual or in-person events
- Collaborate on joint content
- Share best practices and insights
