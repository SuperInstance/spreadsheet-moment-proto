**Why CRDTs enable massive scale:**
- Mathematical guarantee of convergence (no conflicts)
- Peer-to-peer possible (no central bottleneck)
- Offline-first architecture (sync when connection available)
- Immutable operations (perfect audit trail)
- Composable data types (build complex structures)

**Engineering challenges:**
1. **Performance:** CRDTs can be computationally expensive
   - Solution: Optimized Rust implementation
   - Custom CRDT for spreadsheet semantics
   - Lazy evaluation and caching

2. **Memory:** Tracking metadata for millions of cells
   - Solution: Efficient binary encoding
   - Columnar storage for metadata
   - Automatic garbage collection

3. **Network:** Syncing 10,000 clients in real-time
   - Solution: Edge computing at 300+ locations
   - Hierarchical gossip protocol
   - Differential sync (only send changes)

4. **Consistency:** Global consistency without locks
   - Solution: CRDT mathematical guarantees
   - Causal ordering vector clocks
   - Event sourcing for replay

**Performance numbers:**
- 10,000 concurrent users: <50ms latency p95
- 1,000,000 rows: <100ms query response

## Pitch Best Practices

### Do's

1. **Personalize every pitch** - Reference their recent work
2. **Keep it short** - 200 words maximum for initial pitch
3. **Focus on their audience** - Why their readers care
4. **Offer exclusive access** - Embargoes, demos, interviews
5. **Follow up strategically** - 3-5 days, 7-10 days, 14 days
6. **Be responsive** - Reply within 1 hour to interested journalists
7. **Provide assets** - Screenshots, videos, data, customer stories
8. **Respect their time** - Ask if they're interested, don't assume
9. **Be helpful** - Even if they don't cover, offer to be a resource
10. **Build relationships** - Think long-term, not just one story

### Don'ts

1. **Don't send mass emails** - Always personalize
2. **Don't follow up too frequently** - Space it out
3. **Don't be pushy** - Respect their decisions
4. **Don't ignore their beat** - Pitch relevant stories only
5. **Don't bury the lead** - Get to the point quickly
6. **Don't use jargon** - Keep language simple
7. **Don't overpromise** - Only offer what you can deliver
8. **Don't ghost after coverage** - Thank them and maintain relationship
9. **Don't pitch competitors simultaneously** - Exclusivity matters
10. **Don't take rejection personally** - It's not about you
