## Pricing Tiers Summary

### Free Tier ($0/month)
**Target:** Individuals, students
**Limits:** 3 spreadsheets, 100 MB storage
**Features:** Basic AI, community support
**Goal:** Product adoption

### Pro Tier ($12/month or $120/year)
**Target:** Power users, freelancers
**Features:** Unlimited spreadsheets, advanced AI, API access
**Storage:** 10 GB
**Support:** Email (48-hour response)
**ARPU:** $12/month

### Team Tier ($49/user/month or $490/user/year)
**Target:** Startups, SMB teams
**Features:** Real-time collaboration, team admin
**Storage:** 100 GB per user
**Support:** Priority (24-hour response)
**ARPU:** $245/month (5-user team average)

### Enterprise Tier (Custom)
**Target:** Mid-market to enterprise
**Features:** SSO, unlimited storage, dedicated support
**Pricing:** $100+/user/year starting point
**Support:** 24/7 dedicated CSM
**ARPU:** $150/month (weighted average)

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Philosophy
1. **Value-Based:** Price based on customer value realization
2. **Transparent:** Simple, predictable, no hidden fees
3. **Fair:** Price commensurate with value delivered
4. **Growth-Oriented:** Incentivizes adoption and expansion

### Pricing Psychology
- **Anchor Pricing:** Enterprise tier makes Team feel valuable
- **Decoy Effect:** Annual pricing makes monthly expensive
- **Bundling:** Features increase perceived value
- **Loss Aversion:** Trial expiration creates urgency

### Conversion Strategy
**Free → Pro (5% conversion)**
- Triggers: Hit 3 spreadsheets, need advanced AI, remove watermarks

**Pro → Team (15% annual upgrade)**
- Triggers: Need collaboration, team growth, advanced permissions

**Team → Enterprise (10% annual upgrade)**
- Triggers: 100+ users, need SSO, compliance requirements

## Key Assumptions & Risks

### Key Assumptions
1. **Market Demand:** Growing demand for AI-powered productivity tools
2. **Competition:** Excel/Google Sheets don't add comparable AI in 18 months
3. **Technology:** AI capabilities continue to improve, costs decrease
4. **Adoption:** Teams willing to switch from familiar tools
5. **Pricing:** Customers value AI at $12-49/user/month

### Key Risks
1. **Competition:** Microsoft/Google add AI features
2. **Platform Risk:** Dependency on third-party AI APIs
3. **Adoption Friction:** Switching costs from existing tools
4. **Price Sensitivity:** Economic downturns affect software budgets
5. **Data Privacy:** Enterprise concerns about AI data handling

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Differentiation:** Focus on unique AI capabilities
2. **Multi-Cloud:** Diversify AI API dependencies
3. **Migration Tools:** Reduce switching friction
4. **Value Pricing:** Tie to measurable productivity gains
5. **Security First:** SOC 2 compliance, transparent practices
