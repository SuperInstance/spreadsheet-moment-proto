## Troubleshooting Guides

### Diagnostic Framework

**Step 1: Issue Identification**
```
1. Understand the problem
   - What is the issue?
   - When does it occur?
   - What are the symptoms?
   - What is the impact?

2. Gather information
   - User account details
   - Browser and version
   - Operating system
   - Error messages
   - Screenshots/videos
   - Steps to reproduce
```

**Step 2: Initial Assessment**
```
1. Check system status
   - status.spreadsheetmoment.com
   - Known issues page
   - Incident history

2. Verify user environment
   - Browser compatibility
   - Network connectivity
   - Account status
   - Permission levels

3. Search knowledge base
   - Similar issues
   - Known solutions
   - Workarounds
   - Related articles
```

**Step 3: Resolution Attempt**
```
1. Apply known solution
   - Follow guide steps
   - Test solution
   - Verify resolution

2. Document results
   - What worked
   - What didn't work
   - Additional findings
   - User feedback
```

**Step 4: Escalation if Needed**
```
1. Identify need for escalation
   - Solution not available
   - Bug identified
   - Feature request
   - Complexity beyond scope

2. Prepare escalation package
   - Issue summary
   - Steps taken
   - User impact
   - Urgency level
   - Supporting data
```

## FAQ Database

### Account Management

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Security > Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password and then create a new one.

**Q: Can I merge two accounts?**
A: Currently, we don't support account merging. However, you can export data from one account and import it to another.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm this action. Note: This is irreversible and all data will be permanently deleted.

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Profile > Email. You'll need to verify your new email address before the change takes effect.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't receive it within 10 minutes.

---

### Features and Usage

**Q: What's the difference between AI cells and regular cells?**
A: AI cells contain intelligent agents that can reason, predict, and adapt. They can process natural language, learn from data, and make decisions. Regular cells contain static values or traditional formulas.

**Q: How many people can collaborate on a spreadsheet?**
A: Up to 100 people can collaborate on a single spreadsheet simultaneously, depending on your plan. Free plans support up to 10 collaborators.

**Q: Can I use Spreadsheet Moment offline?**
A: Yes! The desktop app works offline and syncs when you reconnect. Changes are saved locally and automatically synchronized.

**Q: What file formats can I import?**
A: We support Excel (.xlsx, .xls), CSV, ODS, and Google Sheets. We also support copy-paste from other sources.

**Q: How do I share a spreadsheet?**
A: Click the Share button, enter email addresses, set permissions (view, comment, or edit), and click Send. You can also create a shareable link.

---

### Billing and Plans

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

**Q: Can I change my plan later?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, with prorated charges or credits.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial of our Pro and Team plans. No credit card required.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?**
A: Yes! We offer 50% off for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@spreadsheetmoment.com for details.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. Contact support@spreadsheetmoment.com for refunds.

---

### Technical Support

**Q: My spreadsheet is loading slowly. What can I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Check your internet connection
4. Reduce spreadsheet size if very large
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact support if problem persists

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What do I do?**
A: Note the error code and message, then:
1. Check our error message database
2. Search the knowledge base
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Clear your browser cache
5. Contact support with error details

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: We support the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. For the best experience, we recommend Chrome.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: You can report bugs through:
- GitHub Issues (for technical users)
- Support email: support@spreadsheetmoment.com
- In-app feedback form
- Community forums

Include steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual behavior, and your environment details.
