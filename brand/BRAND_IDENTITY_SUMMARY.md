## 🎯 Core Brand Elements Established

### Brand Positioning
- **Positioning Statement:** "For data professionals who need powerful computational tools, Spreadsheet Moment is the next-generation spreadsheet platform that combines AI-powered intelligence with tensor-based computation."

- **Market Position:** "The intelligent evolution of spreadsheets"
- **Primary UVP:** "Spreadsheet Moment transforms the familiar spreadsheet interface into an intelligent computational engine"

### Brand Personality
- **Archetype:** The Sage + The Magician
- **Primary Traits:** Intelligent (40%), Approachable (30%), Empowering (20%), Reliable (10%)
- **Voice:** Brilliant, approachable computational companion

### Target Audience
- **6 Detailed Personas:** Research scientists, financial analysts, data science managers, engineering leads, small business owners, graduate students
- **Anti-Personas:** Casual users, pure coders, enterprise traditionalists

### Visual Identity
- **Brand Colors:**
  - Primary: Moment Indigo (#4F46E5)
  - Secondary: Insight Teal (#0D9488)
  - Accent: Discovery Amber (#F59E0B)

- **Typography:**
  - Primary: Inter
  - Secondary: JetBrains Mono
  - Accent: Cal Sans

- **Logo:** "The Insight Grid" - Represents convergence of spreadsheets, AI, and breakthrough insights

- **Icons:** Geometric, 24px grid, 2px stroke, rounded corners

### Design System
- **Tokens:** Complete design token system (colors, typography, spacing, motion, shadows)
- **Components:** Button, Input, Card, Modal, Table, and more
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant throughout
