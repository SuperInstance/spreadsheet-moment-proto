## Visual Identity

### Our Logo

#### Primary Logo
The Spreadsheet Moment logo represents the convergence of spreadsheets, AI intelligence, and breakthrough insights.

**Clear Space:** Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" in "MOMENT" on all sides.

**Minimum Size:** 24px height (digital), 1" height (print)

#### Logo Variations

1. **Horizontal Logo** - Primary usage, icon + wordmark
2. **Stacked Logo** - Constrained spaces, icon over wordmark
3. **Icon Only** - App icons, favicons, social avatars
4. **Wordmark Only** - Editorial contexts

#### Color Variations

1. **Full Color** - Primary usage
2. **Monochrome** - Single-color applications
3. **Reversed** - Light backgrounds (white logo on dark)
4. **Reversed (Dark)** - Dark backgrounds (colored logo on dark)

#### Logo Misuse

❌ Don't stretch or distort
❌ Don't change colors
❌ Don't add effects or filters
❌ Don't rotate or skew
❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds
❌ Don't recreate or modify

### Our Typography

#### Font Families

**Inter** - Primary typeface
- Usage: UI text, body copy, buttons, labels
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700

**JetBrains Mono** - Secondary typeface
- Usage: Code, formulas, technical content
- Weights: 400, 500

**Cal Sans** - Accent typeface
- Usage: Headlines, marketing materials
- Weights: 600, 700

#### Type Scale

- Display 1: 72px - Hero headlines
- Display 2: 60px - Page titles
- Heading 1: 40px - Primary page titles
- Heading 2: 32px - Major sections
- Heading 3: 24px - Subsections
- Heading 4: 20px - Card titles
- Body: 16px - Primary content
- Body Small: 14px - Secondary content
- Caption: 12px - Supporting information

### Our Icons

#### Icon Style
- Geometric, 24px grid

- Rounded corners (2px radius)
- Minimal complexity

#### Icon Categories
- Spreadsheet & computational icons
- AI & intelligence icons
- Action & navigation icons
- Status & feedback icons
- Hardware & performance icons

## Voice and Tone

### Our Voice

**Spreadsheet Moment is your brilliant, approachable computational companion.**

#### Voice Attributes

**Intelligent (40%)**
- Smart, capable, knowledgeable
- Never condescending
- Makes users feel smarter

**Approachable (30%)**
- Friendly, welcoming
- Not intimidating
- Speaks your language

**Empowering (20%)**
- Enables capability
- Unlocks potential
- Removes barriers

**Reliable (10%)**
- Dependable, consistent
- Professional quality
- Trustworthy

### Tone Guidelines

#### Educational Content
- **Tone:** Patient, explanatory, encouraging
- **Example:** "Let's break this down step by step..."

#### Technical Documentation
- **Tone:** Precise, concise, authoritative
- **Example:** "To configure tensor operations..."

#### Marketing Messages
- **Tone:** Energetic, inspiring, confident
- **Example:** "Transform your data into insights instantly"

#### Error Messages
- **Tone:** Helpful, non-blaming, constructive
- **Example:** "Let's try that differently. Here's what happened..."

#### Community Engagement
- **Tone:** Inclusive, appreciative, collaborative
- **Example:** "Great question! Here's how the community approaches this..."

### Writing Style

#### Do's
✅ Use clear, conversational language
✅ Explain technical concepts accessibly
✅ Show enthusiasm for discoveries
✅ Admit when we don't know something
✅ Celebrate user successes
✅ Be concise and direct

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't be condescending or elitist
❌ Don't use jargon unnecessarily
❌ Don't sacrifice clarity for cleverness
❌ Don't use generic responses
❌ Don't hide behind corporate language
❌ Don't be unprofessional

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo Usage

#### Do's
✅ Use SVG format for digital applications
✅ Maintain aspect ratio (never stretch)
✅ Use appropriate variation for context
✅ Ensure sufficient contrast
✅ Maintain clear space
✅ Use official logo files only

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
❌ Don't change logo colors
❌ Don't add effects or filters
❌ Don't rotate or skew the logo
❌ Don't place logo on busy backgrounds
❌ Don't create your own logo variations

### Color Usage

#### Do's
✅ Use semantic colors for their intended purpose
✅ Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text
✅ Test in both light and dark modes
✅ Use color in combination with indicators
✅ Consider colorblind accessibility

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't use color as the only indicator
❌ Don't use semantic colors decoratively
❌ Don't create new colors outside the palette
❌ Don't use low-contrast colors for important information
❌ Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning

### Typography Usage

#### Do's
✅ Use designated font families
✅ Follow the type scale
✅ Maintain appropriate line heights
✅ Ensure sufficient contrast ratios
✅ Test across devices and platforms

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't use unauthorized font families
❌ Don't create arbitrary font sizes
❌ Don't use tight line heights for body text
❌ Don't sacrifice readability for style
❌ Don't use display fonts for body copy

### Icon Usage

#### Do's
✅ Use icons for their intended purpose
✅ Maintain consistent sizing
✅ Provide text labels for icon-only buttons
✅ Ensure sufficient color contrast
✅ Test icon recognition with users

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't use icons as decoration only
❌ Don't mix icon styles
❌ Don't rotate icons unless intended
❌ Don't create custom icons without approval
❌ Don't use icons without clear meaning

## Brand Protection

### Trademark Usage

**Spreadsheet Moment™** is a trademark of SuperInstance.

#### Proper Usage
- Use ™ symbol on first mention in marketing materials
- Capitalize "Spreadsheet Moment"
- Don't modify the name (e.g., SpreadsheetMo, SpreadMoment)
- Don't use as a verb (e.g., "Spreadsheet Moment your data")

#### Domain Names
- Official: spreadsheetmoment.com
- Don't register confusingly similar domains
- Don't use in subdomains deceptively

### Third-Party Usage

#### Partners
- Written approval required
- Use official logo files
- Follow brand guidelines
- Link to official website

#### Press
- Use official press kit only
- Follow trademark guidelines
- Credit appropriately
- Contact for approval

#### Community
- Use official assets when possible
- Follow brand guidelines
- Don't imply endorsement
- Respect trademark rights
