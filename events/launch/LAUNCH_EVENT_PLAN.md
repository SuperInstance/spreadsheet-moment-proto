## 4. Q&A Preparation

### 4.1 Anticipated Questions & Answers

#### Product Questions

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT is one-shot - it forgets everything after each conversation. Spreadsheet Moment creates persistent agents that live in your spreadsheet, learn your workflows, and improve over time. It's the difference between renting a calculator and owning an automated assistant.

**Q: Can I see how the agent makes decisions?**
A: Absolutely - full transparency is core to our platform. Click any agent to see its reasoning, data sources, and learning history. You can audit every step for compliance and verification.

**Q: What if the agent makes a mistake?**
A: Two things happen: First, you can inspect exactly why it made the decision. Second, you correct it once, and the agent learns from that correction - it won't make the same mistake twice. That's the power of learning agents.

**Q: Does this work offline?**
A: After the initial teaching phase, agents run locally on your computer for privacy and speed. They only need internet when learning new patterns or accessing external data.

**Q: What platforms do you support?**
A: We're launching with Excel and Google Sheets support, with Airtable coming soon. One agent works across all platforms - create in Excel, use in Sheets.

#### Business Questions

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: We have a freemium model. Free tier for individuals with basic features. Pro tier at $15/month for power users. Team tier at $49/user/month for collaboration features. Enterprise for large organizations.

**Q: What's your traction?**
A: We're launching today with 5,000 users from our waitlist and beta program. 1,000+ paying customers. And we're just getting started.

**Q: Who are your competitors?**
A: Microsoft Copilot, various ChatGPT wrappers, and formula generators. But none of them offer persistent learning agents - that's our key differentiator and moat.

**Q: What if Microsoft adds this to Excel?**
A: First, we're cross-platform - Microsoft won't build for Google Sheets. Second, we're going deep on features power users want. Third, our agent marketplace creates network effects. And finally, being first-to-market with a strong brand matters.

**Q: Are you raising money?**
A: We've raised $2M in seed funding to get to launch. We're focused on product-market fit before our Series A.

#### Technical Questions

**Q: What LLMs do you use?**
A: We use GPT-4 and Claude for the teaching phase, then distill that knowledge into tiny, efficient agents that run locally. This gives you the power of large models with the speed and privacy of local execution.

**Q: How do you handle data privacy?**
A: User data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Agents run locally on your device. We're SOC2 Type II compliant. And we offer enterprise deployments with on-premise options.

**Q: Can I export my agents?**
A: Yes - agents are portable. Export them as JSON, share them with your team, or even run them on your own infrastructure with our enterprise tier.

**Q: What's your tech stack?**
A: Frontend: JavaScript/TypeScript for spreadsheet integrations. Backend: Python for agent orchestration. Infrastructure: AWS, PostgreSQL, Redis. AI: GPT-4, Claude, custom distillation pipeline.

#### Vision Questions

**Q: Where do you see this in 5 years?**
A: AI agents in every spreadsheet. A thriving marketplace with 100,000+ community-created agents. Expansion beyond spreadsheets to presentations, documents, and databases. And potentially acquisition by a platform company.

**Q: Why did you start this company?**
A: Personal frustration - I was wasting hours every week repeating the same spreadsheet tasks. Current AI tools just added to the friction. I knew there had to be a better way - persistent agents that learn and remember.

**Q: What's your long-term vision?**
A: Democratizing AI automation. Making powerful AI accessible to anyone who knows how to use a spreadsheet. No coding required. Just natural language and examples.

### 4.2 Difficult Questions & Responses

**Q: Isn't this just another AI wrapper?**
A: Not at all - we've built novel technology for persistent agent learning. The wrapper is just the UI. The real innovation is our agent lifecycle, learning system, and distillation pipeline. We're happy to do a technical deep-dive.

**Q: Will users trust AI with financial data?**
A: That's why transparency is our #1 feature. Users can inspect every agent decision. We have audit trails. We're SOC2 compliant. And we're seeing strong adoption in finance roles because they can verify everything.

**Q: What if the AI hallucinates?**
A: Our agents are constrained to spreadsheet operations - they can't hallucinate numbers that don't exist. But if they make a wrong inference, the learning system corrects it permanently. And full transparency means you always see the reasoning.

**Q: Why should I pay for this when ChatGPT is free?**
A: ChatGPT is free because you're training OpenAI's models. With Spreadsheet Moment, you're building your own persistent library of automation. 10 hours saved per month = hundreds of dollars in value for $15/month.

**Q: What's your exit strategy?**
A: We're building a durable, independent company. But realistically, this could be strategic for Microsoft, Google, or Atlassian. We'll cross that bridge when we come to it. For now, we're focused on growth.
