### A/B Testing Framework

**Test Variables:**
- Tweet length (short vs long)
- Thread length (5 vs 10 vs 20 tweets)
- Posting times (morning vs afternoon vs evening)
- Content types (educational vs entertaining vs promotional)
- CTAs (strong vs subtle)
- Hashtag usage (0 vs 3 vs 5 hashtags)
- Visual content (text only vs with images/videos)

**Testing Protocol:**
1. Formulate hypothesis
2. Create test variants
3. Launch simultaneously
4. Measure for 48 hours
5. Analyze results
6. Implement winner
7. Document learnings

## Crisis Management

### Negative Comment Response Protocol

**Tier 1: Constructive Criticism**
- Acknowledge the feedback
- Thank them for the input
- Explain our perspective if helpful
- Invite further discussion

**Tier 2: Frustrated Customer**
- Apologize for their experience
- Move to DMs to resolve
- Document the issue
- Follow up to resolution

**Tier 3: Trolling or Harassment**
- Don't engage publicly
- Block if necessary
- Document for team review
- Maintain professional composure
