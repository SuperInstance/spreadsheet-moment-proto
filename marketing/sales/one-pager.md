## The Problem

Spreadsheets haven't evolved in 40 years. Despite being the world's most ubiquitous business tool, traditional spreadsheets remain:
- **Complex** - 80% of users can't write advanced formulas
- **Isolated** - Limited to <100 simultaneous users
- **Inaccessible** - Completely unusable for 1 billion people with disabilities
- **Risky** - No audit trails, 88% contain critical errors

**The cost:** Enterprises lose $50K+ per year per 100 employees due to spreadsheet errors and inefficiencies.
