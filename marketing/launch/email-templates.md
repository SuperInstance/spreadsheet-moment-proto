## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Optimization
- **Length:** 40-50 characters optimal
- **Personalization:** Use recipient's name when possible
- **Urgency:** Launch announcements, limited-time offers
- **Benefit:** Clear value proposition
- **Curiosity:** Intriguing statements that invite opens

### Preheader Text
- **Length:** 80-100 characters
- **Purpose:** Complement subject line
- **Content:** Key benefit or teaser

### Sending Schedule
- **Day 0:** Launch announcement (all lists)
- **Day 0:** Product Hunt (all lists)
- **Day 3:** Feature deep dive (engaged users)
- **Day 7:** Accessibility focus (all lists)
- **Day 10:** Global collaboration (enterprise)
- **Day 14:** Enterprise security (enterprise)
- **Day 21:** Migration guide (trial users)
- **Day 28:** Customer story (all lists)
- **Day 35:** Open source announcement (developers, technical)
- **Day 45:** Wrap-up & roadmap (all lists)

### Segmentation
- **Waitlist:** Full launch sequence
- **Trial users:** Onboarding focus, conversion emphasis
- **Customers:** Feature announcements, success stories
- **Enterprise:** Security, compliance, ROI focus
- **Developers:** Open source, API, technical content
- **Press/Partners:** News, milestones, partnership opportunities

### Metrics to Track
- **Open rate:** Target 25%+
- **Click rate:** Target 3%+
- **Conversion rate:** Target 1%+
- **Unsubscribe rate:** Keep below 1%
- **Spam complaints:** Keep below 0.1%

### Testing
- **A/B test subject lines** for every send
- **Test send times** (Tuesday/Wednesday 10am PT typically best)
- **Test content variations** (short vs long, different CTAs)
- **Test personalization** (name vs. no name)

### Compliance
- **CAN-SPAM compliant** (US)
- **GDPR compliant** (EU)
- **Unsubscribe link** in every email
- **Physical mailing address** in footer
- **Clear opt-in** for marketing emails
