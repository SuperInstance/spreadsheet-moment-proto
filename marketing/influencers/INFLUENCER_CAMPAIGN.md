# Spreadsheet Moment - Influencer Campaign Strategy

**Version:** 1.0
**Campaign Duration:** 30 Days (Pre-launch through Sustained Momentum)
**Target Influencers:** 50-100
**Expected Reach:** 500K-1M
**Expected Conversions:** 500-1,000 signups

---

## Executive Summary

**Campaign Objective:** Leverage influencer partnerships to amplify launch visibility, build credibility, and drive user acquisition across tech, productivity, and spreadsheet communities.

**Core Strategy:** Partner with micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) who have high engagement in target communities. Provide genuine value through custom content rather than paid placements.

**Key Metrics:**
- Influencer ROI: 3-5x on investment
- Conversion rate: 2-5% from influencer traffic
- Engagement rate: >5% on influencer content
- Viral coefficient: >0.3 from shares

- Engagement rate: >5% (likes + comments / views)

- Audience trust: High authenticity, community respect

- YouTube: Long-form tutorials, reviews
- Twitter/X: Threads, insights, community
- LinkedIn: Thought leadership, business value
- TikTok: Quick tips, productivity hacks

**Geographic Fit:**
- Primary: US, UK, Canada, Australia (English-speaking)
- Secondary: Western Europe (with translation)
- Tertiary: Global tech community (English content)

**Growth Trajectory:**
- Growing audience (not stagnant)
- Increasing engagement
- Consistent posting schedule
- Expanding content formats

**Professionalism:**
- Reliable communication

## 3. Outreach Strategy & Templates

### 3.1 Outreach Sequence

#### Step 1: Warm-Up (1-2 Weeks Before Outreach)
- Follow them on all platforms
- Engage with their content (genuine comments)
- Share their content (when relevant)
- Reply to their stories/posts
- Build recognition

#### Step 2: Initial Outreach (Personalized DM/Email)
**Subject Line:** Collaboration opportunity: Spreadsheet Moment + [Influencer Name]

**Email Template:**

```
Hi [Influencer Name],

I'm [Your Name], founder of Spreadsheet Moment. I've been following your [content type] for [time period] and loved your recent [specific content piece].

We're launching the first spreadsheet AI that actually learns and remembers (unlike ChatGPT which forgets everything). After seeing your work on [relevant topic], I think your audience would find this really valuable.

Would you be open to trying it early and creating content if you find it useful?

Happy to provide:
- Early access to the platform
- Custom demo for your audience
- Exclusive discount for your followers
- Affiliate commission (30% first year)

No pressure - we only want to work with creators who genuinely find value in it.

Let me know if you're interested?

Best,
[Your Name]
Founder, Spreadsheet Moment
```

#### Step 3: Follow-Up (3-4 Days Later)
```
Hi [Influencer Name],

Just following up on my previous note about Spreadsheet Moment.

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this brief: We're launching [Date] and would love your feedback before we go live.

If you're interested, here's a 2-minute demo: [Link]

If not, no worries at all!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### Step 4: Value-Add Follow-Up (1 Week Later)
```
Hi [Influencer Name],

We just published a guide on [topic relevant to their audience] that I thought might be useful for your content: [Link]

No ask here - just thought you'd find it valuable!

If you do want to try Spreadsheet Motion before launch, just let me know.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### 3.2 Platform-Specific Outreach

**LinkedIn:**
- Personal connection request with message
- Keep it professional and concise
- Highlight business value
- Offer exclusive data/insights

**Twitter/X:**
- Public reply to their content first
- Then DM for collaboration
- Keep it casual and direct
- Use their preferred communication style

**YouTube:**
- Comment on videos first
- Email via business inquiry
- Provide value upfront
- Clear collaboration proposal

**Instagram/TikTok:**
- DM via professional account
- Brief video intro
- Show platform in action
- Clear value proposition
