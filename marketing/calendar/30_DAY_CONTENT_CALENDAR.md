## 4. Platform-Specific Guidelines

### 4.1 Twitter/X Guidelines

**Posting Strategy:**
- 3-5 tweets/day during launch week
- 2-3 tweets/day momentum weeks
- Mix of original content and engagement
- Use threads for storytelling
- Engage with every reply

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (tips, how-to)
- 30% Promotional (features, offers)
- 20% Community (spotlights, engagement)
- 10% Behind-the-scenes (team, process)

**Best Practices:**
- Tweet during peak hours (9am, 12pm, 3pm, 7pm PST)
- Use visuals (GIFs, images, videos)
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Reply to every comment
- Use relevant hashtags

**Viral Tactics:**
- Controversial takes (thoughtful, not trolling)
- Threads over threads (10-20 tweets)
- Visual data (charts, graphs)
- Celebrity/industry figure mentions
- Timely content (newsjacking)

### 4.2 LinkedIn Guidelines

**Posting Strategy:**
- 1-2 posts/day max
- Long-form content performs best
- Native documents (PDFs)
- Professional tone
- Tag relevant people/companies

**Content Types:**
- Thought leadership articles
- Case studies
- Data-driven insights
- Professional how-to guides
- Company announcements

**Best Practices:**
- Post Tuesday-Thursday (highest engagement)
- 10am-12pm PST best time
- Use professional imagery
- Include clear CTA
- Engage with comments

**Native Documents:**
- PDF guides
- Slide decks
- Infographics
- White papers
- Case studies

### 4.3 YouTube Guidelines

**Content Strategy:**
- 2-3 videos/week during launch
- Tutorial focus
- SEO-optimized titles
- Consistent branding
- Community tab engagement

**Video Types:**
- Product tutorials (15-20 min)
- Quick tips (3-5 min)
- Case studies (10-15 min)
- Live streams (60 min)
- Shorts (60 sec)

**Optimization:**
- Keyword-rich titles
- Custom thumbnails
- Detailed descriptions
- Tags and hashtags
- End screens and cards

**Community:**
- Respond to all comments
- Pin important comments
- Community posts
- Premiere new videos
- Members-only content

### 4.4 Blog Guidelines

**Content Strategy:**
- 3 posts/week
- SEO focus
- Long-form (1,500-2,500 words)
- Visual content
- Clear CTAs

**Content Types:**
- How-to guides
- Case studies
- Technical deep-dives

- Company updates

**SEO Best Practices:**
- Keyword research
- Optimized headlines

**Distribution:**

### 4.5 Email Guidelines

**Sending Strategy:**
- 2-3 emails/week launch month
- Segmentation by user type
- A/B test subject lines
- Mobile-optimized
- Clear unsubscribe

**Email Types:**
- Product announcements
- Educational content
- Customer stories
- Webinar invitations
- Special offers

**Best Practices:**
- Personalization (first name)
- Single CTA per email
