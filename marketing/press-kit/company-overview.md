# Company Fact Sheet - Spreadsheet Moment

## Company Overview

**Founded:** 2024

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Transform how the world works with data by making spreadsheets intelligent, collaborative, and accessible to everyone.

**Vision:** A world where anyone, anywhere can harness the power of data—regardless of technical expertise, language, or ability.

---

## Product Overview

**What:** AI-native spreadsheet platform with intelligent agents in every cell

**Key Differentiators:**
- AI agents understand natural language (no formulas required)
- Real-time collaboration for 10,000+ simultaneous users
- 37 languages with real-time translation
- Origin-centric data security (complete audit trails)
- 98% WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance
- Open source core with self-hosting option

**Target Customers:**
- Small to mid-sized businesses (Team/Business plans)
- Enterprise organizations (Enterprise plan)
- Individual users and freelancers (Free plan)
- Developers and technical teams (API access)
- Organizations requiring accessibility compliance
- Global teams with language diversity
