# Press Release - Spreadsheet Moment Launch

## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

### Spreadsheet Moment Launches AI-Powered Spreadsheets That Think for Themselves

**Revolutionary platform transforms static spreadsheets into intelligent collaborators with real-time collaboration for 10,000+ users, 37-language support, and enterprise-grade security**

**SAN FRANCISCO** – **March 15, 2026** – Spreadsheet Moment today emerged from stealth with $50M in Series A funding to launch the first AI-native spreadsheet platform that puts intelligent agents in every cell. The platform enables real-time collaboration for 10,000+ simultaneous users across 37 languages while maintaining enterprise-grade security and 98% accessibility compliance.

Spreadsheets haven't fundamentally evolved in 40 years. Despite being the world's most ubiquitous business tool, traditional spreadsheets remain static, error-prone, and isolated. Spreadsheet Moment reimagines the spreadsheet from the ground up as an intelligent, collaborative, and globally accessible platform.

**Key Innovations:**

- **AI Agents in Every Cell:** Users interact with spreadsheets using natural language instead of complex formulas. Agents understand context, make suggestions, and learn from user behavior.
- **Unlimited Real-Time Collaboration:** 10,000+ users can work simultaneously in the same spreadsheet with sub-50ms synchronization—100x more users than traditional platforms.
- **True Multilingual Support:** Full native support for 37 languages with real-time translation, allowing global teams to work together without language barriers.
- **Origin-Centric Data Security:** Every data point tracks its source, modification history, and approval status—built for SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliance.
- **Universal Accessibility:** 98% WCAG 2.1 AA compliance makes spreadsheets fully accessible to users with disabilities for the first time.

**"Spreadsheets power the world's businesses, but they're operating on 40-year-old technology,"** said Sarah Chen, CEO and co-founder of Spreadsheet Moment. **"We're not just adding AI features to existing spreadsheets—we're reimagining what a spreadsheet can be when every cell is intelligent, when collaboration happens at any scale, and when anyone, anywhere can use them regardless of language or ability."**

**Early customers report transformative results:**

- **TechCorp** replaced 50 different spreadsheets with one Spreadsheet Moment workspace, reducing forecasting time from 2 days to 2 hours
- **GlobalManufacturing Inc.** unified teams across Tokyo, London, and New York with real-time translation
- **FinanceForward** enabled visually impaired analysts to work independently with full accessibility features

**"We've tried every spreadsheet platform, but Spreadsheet Moment is the first that actually understands our business,"** said Miguel Rodriguez, Operations Director at GlobalManufacturing Inc. **"The AI agents don't just crunch numbers—they anticipate what we need."**

**Platform Capabilities:**

- **Agent-Based Intelligence:** Natural language processing, predictive suggestions, self-healing formulas
- **Enterprise-Grade Security:** SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, origin-centric data tracking, SSO/SAML integration
- **Developer-Friendly:** Open source core, API-first design, custom agent marketplace
- **Global Scale:** 300+ edge locations worldwide, <50ms latency p95, 99.99% uptime SLA
- **Cross-Platform:** Web, desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux), and mobile (iOS, Android) applications

**Pricing and Availability:**

Spreadsheet Moment is available immediately at spreadsheetmoment.com with three tiers:

- **Free Forever:** Up to 5 users, basic AI agents, 5 languages
- **Team:** $12 per user/month: Advanced AI agents, 37 languages, priority support
- **Business:** $29 per user/month: Custom agents, SSO/SAML, unlimited storage, SLA guarantee
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing: Self-hosted deployment, compliance reviews, 24/7 support

**The $50M Series A round was led by Sequoia Capital with participation from Accel and Index Ventures, bringing total funding to $65M.** The company will use the funds to expand R&D, grow the team, and scale global infrastructure.

**"Every business runs on spreadsheets, but spreadsheets haven't kept up with how businesses work today—global teams, real-time collaboration, AI-powered insights, and universal accessibility,"** said Alfred Lin, Partner at Sequoia Capital. **"Spreadsheet Moment isn't just improving spreadsheets—it's redefining them for the modern era."**

**About Spreadsheet Moment:**

Spreadsheet Moment is the first AI-native spreadsheet platform that puts intelligent agents in every cell. Founded in 2024 by a team from Google, Microsoft, and Salesforce, Spreadsheet Moment is on a mission to transform how the world works with data. The platform is trusted by 10,000+ organizations worldwide and is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.
