## Overview

Creating your SuperInstance account is your first step toward accessing personalized, cross-cultural educational AI dialogue. This guide will walk you through the simple registration process.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

Navigate to [https://superinstance.ai/register](https://superinstance.ai/register)

### Step 2: Choose Your Account Type

Select the account type that best fits your needs:

- **Individual Learner:** For personal educational growth
- **Educator:** For teachers and instructors
- **Institution:** For schools, universities, and organizations
- **Enterprise:** For businesses and large organizations

### Step 3: Enter Your Information

Fill in the required fields:

**Required Information:**
- Email address
- Password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and number)
- First name
- Last name
- Country/Region

**Optional Information:**
- Phone number
- Organization name
- Date of birth (for age-appropriate content)

### Step 4: Verify Your Email

After submitting the registration form, you'll receive a verification email at the address you provided.

1. Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@superinstance.ai`
2. Click the "Verify Email" button in the email
3. If you don't see the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder
4. Request a new verification email if needed

### Step 5: Complete Your Profile

Once your email is verified, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:

**Language Preferences:**
- Select your primary language
- Choose additional languages you speak
- Set your preferred interface language

**Educational Interests:**
- Select your education level
- Choose subjects of interest
- Indicate your learning goals

**Cultural Preferences:**
- Select your cultural context (individual/collective)
- Choose religious/secular content preference
- Set regional customization options

### Step 6: Start Your First Dialogue

After completing your profile, you'll be taken to the main dashboard where you can:

- Start your first AI dialogue session
- Explore teaching personalities
- Browse educational content
- Customize your learning experience

## Password Requirements

Your password must meet the following criteria:

- **Minimum length:** 8 characters
- **Maximum length:** 128 characters
- **Required:** At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- **Required:** At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- **Required:** At least one number (0-9)
- **Optional:** Special characters (!@#$%^&*)
- **Prohibited:** Your email address or name

### Password Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Use a passphrase (e.g., "BlueHorse$Sky42")
- Use a password manager
- Update your password regularly
- Use unique passwords for each account

❌ **Don't:**
- Use common words or phrases
- Use personal information (birthdays, names)
- Share your password with anyone
- Reuse passwords from other sites

## Troubleshooting

### Email Not Received

If you don't receive the verification email within 5 minutes:

1. **Check your spam folder**
2. **Verify the email address** for typos
3. **Add noreply@superinstance.ai to your contacts**
4. **Request a new verification email** from the login page
5. **Contact support** if the issue persists

### Email Already in Use

If you see "Email already registered":

1. **Try logging in** with that email address
2. **Use "Forgot Password"** if you don't remember your password
3. **Contact support** if you believe this is an error
4. **Use a different email address** to create a new account

### Password Not Accepted

If your password is rejected:

1. **Check the requirements** listed above
2. **Ensure you're using a supported browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. **Clear your browser cache** if the form seems stuck
4. **Try a different browser** if problems persist
