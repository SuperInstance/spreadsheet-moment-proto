## Risk Management

### Identified Risks

**Risk 1: High Bounce Rate (>5%)**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Thorough list cleaning before launch
  - IP warm-up schedule followed
  - Real-time monitoring and quick response
  - Contingency: Pause sends, clean list, resume

**Risk 2: Low Engagement (<20% open rate)**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Strong subject line testing
  - Personalization implementation
  - Segmentation optimization
  - Contingency: Re-engagement campaign

**Risk 3: Spam Complaints (>0.2%)**
- **Probability:** Low
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Permission-based lists only
  - Easy unsubscribe process
  - Relevant, valuable content
  - Contingency: Review content, adjust targeting

**Risk 4: Technical Issues**
- **Probability:** Low
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Thorough testing before launch
  - Technical team on standby
  - Backup systems ready
  - Contingency: Delay launch, fix issues

**Risk 5: Overwhelming Support**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Support team scaling for launch
  - FAQ preparation
  - Automated responses
  - Contingency: Hire temporary support

### Contingency Plans

**If Launch Day Fails:**
1. Immediate pause of all sends
2. Root cause analysis
3. Fix identified issues
4. Reschedule launch within 7 days
5. Communicate delays transparently

**If Performance Below Targets:**
1. Analyze underperforming segments
2. Optimize content and targeting
3. Implement re-engagement campaigns
4. Adjust success targets if needed
5. Document lessons learned

**If Budget Exceeded:**
1. Review spend against ROI
2. Optimize high-cost channels
3. Focus on organic growth
4. Extend timeline if needed
5. Request additional budget if justified
