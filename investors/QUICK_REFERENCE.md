## ⚡ Pro Tips

### Outreach Best Practices
- **Timing:** Best days: Tue-Thu. Best times: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM
- **Follow-Up:** 3-5 business days after initial email
- **Personalization:** Reference their portfolio, recent investments
- **Brevity:** Keep emails under 200 words
- **Mobile-Friendly:** Short paragraphs, clear CTA

### Meeting Best Practices
- **Preparation:** Research investor background, portfolio
- **Opening:** Start with compelling stat or story
- **Energy:** High enthusiasm, confident delivery
- **Interaction:** Ask questions, check understanding
- **Closing:** Clear ask, specific next steps

### Follow-Up Best Practices
- **Speed:** Send follow-up within 24 hours
- **Value:** Include additional materials, data, intros
- **Clarity:** Propose specific next steps
- **Persistence:** 3-4 follow-ups max, then move on
