## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Lower Valuation (Probability: 30%)
**Mitigation:**
- Competitive tension (multiple term sheets)
- Strong traction (68% retention, 52 NPS)
- Comparable valuations (15-30x ARR for AI SaaS)
- Walk-away price ($35M pre-money minimum)

### Risk 2: Smaller Round Size (Probability: 20%)
**Mitigation:**
- Larger investor universe (60+ investors)
- Angel backfill (10-15 angels ready)
- Lower minimum viable raise ($8M vs $10-15M)
- Extend runway (bootstrap longer if needed)

### Risk 3: No Lead Investor (Probability: 15%)
**Mitigation:**
- Multiple strong candidates (5-10 potential leads)
- Syndicate structure (democratized lead)
- Bridge financing (existing investors)
- Delay raise (build more traction)

### Risk 4: Economic Downturn (Probability: 10%)
**Mitigation:**
- Productivity value prop (automation saves money)
- Freemium model (low barrier to entry)
- Capital efficient (24-month runway)
- Extend timeline (raise over longer period)

### Risk 5: Platform Competition (Probability: 60%)
**Mitigation:**
- Cross-platform advantage (Microsoft won't build for Google)
- First-mover brand (18-month head start)
- Marketplace network effects (agent sharing)
- Acquisition option (strategic exit)
