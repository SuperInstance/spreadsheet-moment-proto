## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Optimization
- **Keep it short:** Under 50 characters
- **Personalize:** Include their name or firm
- **Be specific:** Reference conversation/connection
- **Create urgency:** Timeline or milestone
- **Avoid spam:** No "URGENT" or all-caps

### Body Optimization
- **Lead with value:** Traction, metrics, progress
- **Keep it brief:** Under 200 words
- **Clear CTA:** One specific ask
- **Mobile-friendly:** Short paragraphs
- **Professional but warm:** Show personality

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (local time)
- **Avoid:** Mondays (busy), Fridays (distracted)
- **Follow up:** 3-5 business days if no response

### Follow-Up Strategy
1. **Initial email**
2. **Follow up 3-5 days later** (bump to top)
3. **Second follow-up 7 days later** (add value/update)
4. **Third follow-up 14 days later** (break up/close)
5. **Move on** if no response after 3-4 attempts
