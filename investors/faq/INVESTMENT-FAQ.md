## Company & Product

### Q: What does Spreadsheet Moment do?

**A:** Spreadsheet Moment brings persistent AI memory to spreadsheets. Our AI agents learn user workflows, distill into reusable automation, and compound in value over time. Unlike one-shot AI tools that forget everything, our agents never stop learning.

### Q: How is Spreadsheet Moment different from ChatGPT or Microsoft Copilot?

**A:** Three key differences:
1. **Persistent Memory:** Our agents learn and remember across sessions
2. **Cross-Platform:** Works across Excel, Sheets, and Airtable
3. **Full Inspection:** You can see exactly how the agent reached its conclusion

### Q: What problem does Spreadsheet Moment solve?

**A:** 1 billion spreadsheet users waste 4-8 hours per week on repetitive tasks. Current AI tools provide one-shot answers that forget everything. We provide persistent AI agents that learn your work and automate it forever.

### Q: Who is your target customer?

**A:** Primary: FP&A professionals (2M globally). Secondary: Sales operations, marketing analytics, small business. Long-term: All knowledge workers who use spreadsheets.

### Q: How do users interact with Spreadsheet Moment?

**A:** Through natural language in their spreadsheet:
```
=SPREADSHEET_AGENT("Financial Analyst")

User: "Watch me generate this monthly report"
AI: [Learns the pattern]
AI: "I've created an agent that does this automatically. It runs in 0.2 seconds."
```

### Q: What platforms do you support?

**A:** Excel (add-in), Google Sheets (add-on), Airtable (extension). Coming soon: Notion, Coda, and other productivity tools.

## Technology & IP

### Q: What is your technology stack?

**A:**
- **Frontend:** React 18 + TypeScript
- **Backend:** Node.js + TypeScript
- **AI:** GPT-4 (primary), Claude 3 (secondary), Gemini Pro (backup)
- **Infrastructure:** AWS, Cloudflare Workers, Google Cloud
- **Database:** PostgreSQL, Redis

### Q: How does distillation work?

**A:** We observe the LLM (GPT-4) performing a task, extract patterns, and distill into a smaller, faster model:
- **LLM:** 10 seconds, $0.10 per run
- **Distilled:** 0.2 seconds, $0.0001 per run
- **Result:** 50-1000x speedup, 100-1000x cost reduction

### Q: What is your IP strategy?

**A:**
- **Patents Pending:** 3 patents filed (distillation, orchestration, persistence)
- **Trademarks:** "Spreadsheet Moment" (pending), "POLLN" (registered)
- **Open Source:** POLLN Framework (MIT license) drives adoption
- **Proprietary:** Platform features and distillation engine

### Q: How do you ensure data privacy and security?

**A:**
- **Local-first:** Process data locally when possible
- **Encryption:** AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Compliance:** SOC2 Type II (planned Q4 2026), GDPR (planned Q2 2026)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, audit logs
- **Enterprise:** On-premise deployment option

### Q: What is your technical differentiation?

**A:**
1. **Incremental Distillation:** Proprietary algorithm
2. **Confidence Scoring:** Three-zone model (GREEN/YELLOW/RED)
3. **Multi-Agent Orchestration:** Parallel execution
4. **Hybrid Execution:** Local first, cloud fallback
5. **Accessibility:** 98% WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

### Q: How do you handle errors and edge cases?

**A:**
- **Confidence Scoring:** Only use agents when >90% confident
- **Fallback:** Escalate to LLM for low confidence
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** Require verification for high-stakes calculations
- **Audit Trails:** Complete traceability for compliance
