**Effectiveness:** ✅ Strong (70% accept, 20% neutral, 10% still concerned)

## Pipeline Management Best Practices

### Daily Tasks
- **Morning:** Check for new emails/messages from investors
- **Mid-Day:** Respond to all investor inquiries within 24 hours
- **Evening:** Update pipeline tracker with new information

### Weekly Tasks
- **Monday Morning:** Review pipeline, set weekly goals
- **Wednesday:** Check on outstanding follow-ups
- **Friday Afternoon:** Update pipeline dashboard, prepare weekly report

### Monthly Tasks
- **Start of Month:** Review pipeline health, adjust strategy
- **Middle of Month:** Analyze conversion rates, optimize process
- **End of Month:** Evaluate progress against targets, forecast next month

### Pipeline Maintenance
- **Data Quality:** Keep all records up-to-date
- **Follow-Up:** Never let an investor go more than 7 days without contact
- **Documentation:** Record all meetings, feedback, next steps
- **Analysis:** Regularly analyze what's working, what's not
