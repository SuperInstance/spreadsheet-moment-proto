# Business Model

## SaaS + Marketplace Revenue

### Revenue Streams

**1. Subscription SaaS (Primary)**
```
Free Tier:      $0/month      (Viral growth)
Pro Tier:       $15/month     (Individual power users)
Team Tier:      $49/user/mo   (Small teams)
Enterprise:     $100-250/user/mo (Large orgs)
```

**2. Agent Marketplace (Secondary)**
```
Year 1:         $0            (No marketplace)
Year 2:         $2M ARR       (10% of total)
Year 3:         $24M ARR      (20% of total)
Year 4:         $30M ARR      (25% of total)
```

### Unit Economics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Content marketing: $200
- Product Hunt: $0 (one-time)
- App stores: $0 (organic)
- Referrals: $0 (viral)
- **Blended CAC: $50**

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Average tenure: 24 months
- Average ARPU: $20/month
- **LTV = $480**

**Unit Economics:**
- **LTV:CAC = 9.6x** (Target: >3x)
- **Payback = 2.5 months** (Target: <12 months)
- **Gross margin = 80%** (Target: >70%)

### Marketplace Economics

**Creator Revenue:**
- Agent price: $5-50 one-time OR $2-10/month
- Platform fee: 30%
- Creator keeps: 70%

**Creator Earnings Potential:**
- 100 sales at $10 = $700 creator revenue
- Top creators: $5K-50K/month potential
- Incentivizes high-quality agent development

**POLLN Marketplace Revenue:**
- Year 2: $2M (early stage)
- Year 3: $24M (maturing)
- Year 4: $66M (healthy ecosystem)

### Go-to-Market Strategy

**Phase 1: Beachhead (Months 1-6)**
- Target: 1,000 paying users
- Channels: Content marketing, Product Hunt, influencer partnerships
- Focus: FP&A professionals

**Phase 2: Cross the Chasm (Months 7-18)**
- Target: 50,000 paying users
- Channels: App store optimization, viral mechanics, partnerships
- Focus: Sales ops, marketing analytics

**Phase 3: Scale (Months 19-36)**
- Target: 500,000 paying users
- Channels: Enterprise sales, paid acquisition, community
- Focus: All segments

### Churn & Retention

**Target Metrics:**
- Monthly churn: <5%
- Net dollar retention: 120%+
- Expansion revenue: 20%+

**Churn Reduction:**
- Agent library investment increases switching costs
- Team collaboration creates network effects
- Continuous value delivery via learning agents

### Profitability

**Break-even Analysis:**
- Fixed costs: $1.5M/month at scale
- Variable costs: 20% of revenue
- Gross margin: 80%
- **Break-even: $1.875M/month**
- **Break-even users: 94K**

**Timeline to Profitability:**
- Month 18: Reach 100K users → break even
- Month 24: Reach 1M users → $20M ARR, profitable
- Option for Series B at Month 30 to accelerate growth

**The economics work. The model scales.**
