# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phased Market Penetration

### Strategy Overview

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Phase 1          Phase 2              Phase 3             │
│  (Beachhead)      (Cross the Chasm)    (Scale)             │
│                                                             │
│  Months 1-6       Months 7-18          Months 19-36        │
│                                                             │
│  1K users         50K users            500K users          │
│  FP&A focus       Multi-segment        All segments        │
│  Content + PH     App stores + viral   Enterprise + paid    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Phase 1: Beachhead (Months 1-6)

**Target: 1,000 Paying Users**

**Primary Segment: FP&A Professionals**
- Why: High willingness to pay, clear ROI
- Size: 2M professionals globally
- Pain: Monthly reporting, variance analysis
- Solution: Pre-built report agents

**Channels:**

**Content Marketing (60%):**
- "Watch AI Learn Your Spreadsheet" demo videos
- Twitter/X threads: before/after agent examples
- Reddit: r/Excel, r/spreadsheets, r/financialmodelling
- Weekly blog posts with SEO optimization
- YouTube tutorials

**Product Hunt (20%):**
- Premium placement
- Launch day coordination
- 100 influencer outreach
- Target: Top 5 product of the day

**Influencer Partnerships (20%):**
- Excel YouTube creators (10K-100K subs)
- Notion/Airtable influencers
- "Productivity YouTubers"
- Affiliate commission: 30% first year

**Leverage Existing Community:**
- GitHub stars → early adopters
- Technical blog readers → power users
- Developer-focused content first, then expand

**Tactics:**
- Weekly content calendar
- Monthly YouTube releases
- Daily Twitter engagement
- Product Hunt launch prep

### Phase 2: Cross the Chasm (Months 7-18)

**Target: 50,000 Paying Users**

**Expanded Segments:**
- Sales Operations (500K professionals)
- Marketing Analytics (5M professionals)
- HR Analytics (1M professionals)

**Channels:**

**App Store Optimization (40%):**
- Microsoft AppSource (Excel add-in)
- Google Workspace Marketplace
- Keyword optimization
- A/B testing listings
- Incentivize early reviews

**Viral Mechanics (30%):**
- "Share this agent" creates new users
- Public agent gallery (templates)
- Referral program: Free month for both
- Teams feature creates network effects
- "Made with Spreadsheet Moment" branding

**Partnerships (20%):**
- Spreadsheet training companies
- Corporate training departments
- Consulting firms (build for clients)
- Excel template creators

**Content Expansion (10%):**
- Industry-specific use cases
- Case studies: "How Company X saved 10,000 hours"
- Webinar series: AI for finance professionals

**Tactics:**
- Optimize app store listings
- Launch referral program
- Onboard 10 partners
- Publish 5 case studies

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 19-36)

**Target: 500,000 Paying Users**

**All Segments:**
- Enterprise (F500)
- SMB (10M businesses)
- Prosumer (100M individuals)
- Developers (10M technical users)

**Channels:**

**Enterprise Sales (40%):**
- Direct sales to F500
- Case studies with quantified ROI
- Integration partnerships
- Industry events and conferences

**Paid Acquisition (30%):**
- Search ads: "AI spreadsheet automation"
- LinkedIn ads: Job titles
- Retargeting: Content consumers
- YouTube pre-roll ads

**Community-Led Growth (20%):**
- User conferences/meetups
- Certified agent builder program
- Marketplace revenue share
- Ambassador program

**International Expansion (10%):**
- Localize for EU markets
- APAC expansion
- Regional agent templates

**Tactics:**
- Hire 10 enterprise sales reps
- Launch paid acquisition
- Host first user conference
- Expand to 5 countries

### Viral Mechanics

**Built-in Virality:**
1. **Agent Sharing:** Every shared agent creates new user signups
2. **Public Library:** Templates drive organic discovery
3. **Teams Feature:** Collaboration creates network effects
4. **Referral Program:** Incentivizes word-of-mouth
5. **Brand Watermark:** "Made with Spreadsheet Moment"

**Viral Coefficient:**
- Target K-factor: 0.3-0.5
- Each user brings 0.3-0.5 new users
- Compounds growth over time

### Pricing Strategy

**Freemium Funnel:**
```
Free (Millions) → Pro (100K) → Team (10K) → Enterprise (1K)
     5%             20%           15%           10%
```

**Pricing Psychology:**
- Anchor with Enterprise price ($100+)
- Pro tier at $15 feels like bargain
- Teams tier at $49 seems reasonable
- Yearly billing: 2 months free (increases retention)

### Customer Success

**Onboarding:**
- Interactive tutorial: "Create your first agent in 5 min"
- Template library: Quick time-to-value
- In-app prompts: Progressive disclosure
- Email sequence: Days 1, 3, 7, 14, 30

**Retention:**
- Weekly "agent tips" emails
- Monthly "new templates" announcements
- Quarterly webinars: Advanced techniques
- Personal check-ins: Team tier and above

**Expansion:**
- Seat expansion prompts
- Tier upgrade triggers
- Marketplace recommendations
- Custom agent offers

### Key Metrics & Targets

**Acquisition:**
- Signups: 1K/month (Year 1) → 10K/month (Year 2) → 50K/month (Year 3)
- CAC: <$50 (blended)
- Conversion: Free → Pro >5%

**Engagement:**
- DAU/MAU: >30%
- Agents created: >3/month
- Agent reuse: >60%

**Retention:**
- Day-7: >40%
- Day-30: >20%
- Day-90: >10%
- Monthly churn: <5%

**Virality:**
- K-factor: >0.3
- Share rate: >20%
- Referral conversion: >10%
