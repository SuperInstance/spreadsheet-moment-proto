## Mission: Deep Paper Analysis & Reader Psychology

1. **Mathematical Rigor Analysis**
   - Review all mathematical proofs and derivations

2. **Reader Psychology Research**
   - Study cognitive load factors in technical papers

   - Create reader psychology profile database

3. **Comparative Literature Analysis**
   - Compare our papers to top-tier conference papers
   - Identify formatting and style improvements
   - Analyze successful explanation techniques
   - Document best practices for technical communication
