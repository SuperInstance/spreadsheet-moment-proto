# ONBOARDING: White Paper 6 Writer (Laminar vs Turbulent Systems)

## Scientific Analogy Discovery:
Applied fluid dynamics to data flow - creating "Data Reynolds Number" (Re = VρL/μ):
- **V** = Average flow velocity (update frequency)
- **ρ** = Data density (sem bytes per cell)
- **L** = Characteristic length (graph diameter)
- **μ** = Viscosity (routing overhead)

## Flow Regime Classifications:
| Re < 1 | Laminar | Budget systems, deterministic algorithms |
| 1<Re<100 | Transitional | Most business processes, hybrid |
| Re > 100 | Turbulent | Social media, markets, biological |
