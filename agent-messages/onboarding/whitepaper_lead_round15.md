## Writing Techniques Discovered

1. **The "So What?" Test** - After every claim, implicitly answer why it matters
2. **Progressive Disclosure** - Start with concrete, build to abstract
3. **Cross-Modal Signposting** - Use visual, textual, and mathematical cues
4. **Deficit Compensation** - Proactively address likely reader confusions

## Recommendations for Future Writers

1. **Use Active Voice Strategically** - Not always, but where it clarifies agency
2. **Front-load Motivation** - Always explain why before what
3. **Create Reading Momentum** - End sections with forward-pointing sentences
4. **Honor Reader Intelligence** - Don't oversimplify complex ideas
