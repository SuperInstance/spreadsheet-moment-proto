### 3. Success Metrics and Health Scores

**Location:** `customer-success/metrics/`

**Purpose:** Measure customer health and predict outcomes

**Health Score Components:**
- Product Engagement (40 points)
- Account Strength (25 points)
- Value Realization (20 points)
- Sentiment and Satisfaction (15 points)

**Score Interpretation:**
- 90-100: Excellent (Green) - Very low churn risk
- 70-89: Healthy (Green) - Low churn risk
- 50-69: At Risk (Yellow) - Medium churn risk
- 30-49: Critical (Orange) - High churn risk
- 0-29: Imminent Churn (Red) - Very high churn risk

**Churn Prediction Indicators:**
- Usage decline patterns (50%+ decrease)
- Engagement loss signals (14+ days inactive)
- Sentiment deterioration (NPS drop)
- Account changes (downgrade, user loss)

**Upsell Opportunity Signals:**
- Plan limit approaching (90%+)
- Power user behavior patterns
- Organizational expansion
- High satisfaction scores

### 4. Customer Success Playbooks

**Location:** `customer-success/playbooks/`

**Purpose:** Scenario-based guides for common situations

**Available Playbooks:**

**Trial Conversion Playbook**
- Trigger: Day 21 of trial
- Goal: Convert trial to paid
- Success Rate Target: > 25%
- Key Actions: Assessment, outreach, value reinforcement, conversion support

**User Activation Playbook**
- Trigger: New user signup
- Goal: Achieve time-to-first-value < 15 minutes
- Success Rate Target: > 75% activation
- Key Actions: Welcome, first value achievement, habit formation

**Renewal Playbook**
- Trigger: 90/30/14 days before renewal
- Goal: Secure renewal at current or higher tier
- Success Rate Target: > 90% renewal
- Key Actions: Preparation, value review, proposal, confirmation

**Upsell Playbook**
- Trigger: Upsell readiness score > 80
- Goal: Identify and close expansion opportunities
- Success Rate Target: > 40% conversion
- Key Actions: Qualification, value presentation, objection handling

**At-Risk Intervention Playbook**
- Trigger: Health score drops below 60
- Goal: Re-engage customer and prevent churn
- Success Rate Target: > 60% recovery
- Key Actions: Risk assessment, outreach, value reinforcement, monitoring

**Customer Advocacy Playbook**
- Trigger: NPS score 9-10, high engagement
- Goal: Transform satisfied users into advocates
- Success Rate Target: > 20% of promoters
- Key Actions: Identification, cultivation, activation, recognition
