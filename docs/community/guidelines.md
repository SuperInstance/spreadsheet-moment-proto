# Community Guidelines

Be excellent to each other.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:
- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Other unethical or unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Report violations by:
- Emailing conduct@spreadsheetmoment.com
- DMing moderators on Discord
- Using GitHub's report功能

### Consequences

Responses may include:
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
- Removal of content

## Communication Guidelines

### GitHub

- Search before opening issues
- Use issue templates
- Be specific and concise
- Label issues appropriately

### Discord

- Use appropriate channels
- Avoid spamming
- Respect community roles
- Follow Discord TOS

### Email

- Use descriptive subject lines
- Include relevant context
- Allow 24-48h response time

## Content Guidelines

### Code Contributions

- Follow existing style
- Write clear commit messages
- Update documentation
- Add tests when appropriate

### Documentation

- Use clear language
- Include examples
- Keep it up to date
- Link to related resources

### Support

- Be patient with newcomers
- Provide constructive help
- Admit when you don't know
- Direct to official resources

## Diversity & Inclusion

We value:
- Different perspectives
- Varied experiences
- Unique backgrounds
- Diverse skills

We actively:
- Welcome newcomers
- Mentor contributors
- Celebrate differences
- Learn from each other

## Dispute Resolution

### Step 1: Direct Communication

Try to resolve directly with the other party.

### Step 2: Mediation

Contact a mediator for help.

### Step 3: Escalation

Escalate to the conduct committee.
