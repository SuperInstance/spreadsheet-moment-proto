### Most Memorable Across Cultures:

1. **"SpreadsheetMoment"** variations:
   - Best: Japanese "表の瞬間" (Table's Moment - poetic, concise)
   - Most technical: German "TabellenkalkulationsMoment" (too long)
   - Most universal: Spanish "MomentoDeHojaDeCálculo" (clear but long)

2. **"SpreadingClaw"** variations:
   - Best: Mandarin "扩散爪" (concise, 3 characters)
   - Most playful: Arabic "امتداد المخلب" (Extension of the Claw)
   - Most memorable: Japanese "広がる爪" (simple, visual)

3. **"ClawSheets"** variations:
   - Best: Mandarin "爪表" (2 characters, incredibly concise)
   - Most technical: Japanese "クローシート" (katakana, modern)
   - Most natural: German "KlauenBlätter" (flows well)

## Final Recommendation:

### **Primary Choice: "SpreadsheetMoment"**

**Rationale:**
1. **Universal concept**: Every culture understands spreadsheets
2. **Emotional resonance**: "Moment" implies insight, revelation, breakthrough
3. **Technical accuracy**: Describes exactly what the system does
4. **Translation friendly**: Works well across all languages
5. **Professional yet accessible**: Not too playful, not too academic
6. **Memorable**: Has a nice rhythm in English (spreed-SHEET MO-ment)

**Recommended repository name**: `spreadsheet-moment` (with hyphens for URL friendliness)

**Alternative for more playful branding**: `claw-sheets`
- Shorter, punchier
- References the claw machine visual metaphor
- Easy to say and remember
- "Claw" translates well across cultures (Mandarin "爪" is perfect)

**For international markets**: Consider localizing to:
- Japanese: `table-no-shun` (表の瞬間)
- Mandarin: `zhua-biao` (爪表) or `biao-miao` (表瞬)
- Spanish: `momento-calculo`

### Project Branding Strategy:

Use **"SpreadsheetMoment"** as the primary English name, but incorporate the claw visual metaphor in the logo and imagery. The claw represents:
- The AI reaching into possibility space
- Selecting the perfect solution
- The mechanical, tangible nature of the system

This gives you:
- Professional name for documentation
- Visual metaphor for diagrams
- Cross-cultural appeal
- Technical accuracy
- Memorability
