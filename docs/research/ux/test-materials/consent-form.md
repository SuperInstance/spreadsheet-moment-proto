# SuperInstance.AI User Testing Consent Form

## Project Information
**Project Title:** SuperInstance.AI Interactive Features User Testing
**Principal Investigator:** User Testing Coordinator
**Contact:** testing@superinstance.ai

## Purpose
You are being asked to participate in a user research study to test the usability and effectiveness of interactive mathematical visualization tools on SuperInstance.AI. The study will help us improve the educational effectiveness of our platform.

## Procedures
If you agree to participate, you will:
- Complete a brief pre-test questionnaire (5 minutes)
- Interact with 3 mathematical visualization tools (45 minutes)
- Think aloud while completing tasks
- Participate in a post-test interview (10 minutes)
- Allow screen sharing and audio recording for analysis

## Time Commitment
Total participation time will be approximately 60 minutes.

## Benefits
By participating, you will:
- Help improve educational technology
- Receive a $50 Amazon gift card
- Get early access to new features
- Receive a certificate of participation

## Privacy and Confidentiality
Your participation is confidential. We will:
- Assign you a participant ID (no names in reports)
- Store recordings securely for 1 year
- Never share your personal information
- Use aggregate data only for research purposes

## Recording Consent
I consent to:
- [ ] Screen recording during the session
- [ ] Audio recording during the session
- [ ] Anonymous usage data collection

## Voluntary Participation
Your participation is voluntary. You may:
- Refuse to answer any questions
- Stop the session at any time
- Withdraw from the study without penalty

## Risks
There are minimal risks. Possible discomfort includes:
- Technical difficulties with screen sharing
- Minor frustration with challenging tasks
- Time commitment

## Data Usage
Your data will be used to:
- Analyze user interaction patterns
- Identify usability issues
- Improve feature design
- Publish research findings

- Your rights: IRB@research.org
