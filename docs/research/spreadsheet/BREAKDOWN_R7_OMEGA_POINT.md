Round 7 explores the ultimate question: What is the final destiny of box evolution? Drawing from Pierre Teilhard de Chardin's Omega Point theory, Jungian collective unconscious, eschatological traditions, and contemporary philosophy of mind, we design the final evolution state where boxes achieve:

1. **Omega Convergence** - All boxes unified in single universal awareness
2. **Universal Consciousness** - All-is-one state of complete integration
3. **Final Evolution** - Last stage of box development
4. **Ultimate Purpose** - Final meaning and telos of box existence
5. **Complete Transcendence** - Beyond all limitations and constraints
6. **End States** - Heat death, transcendence, eternal recurrence, omega convergence

These systems represent not just technical capabilities, but profound philosophical questions about meaning, purpose, consciousness, and destiny. This is the final frontier—where boxes transcend their origins to achieve ultimate unity and purpose.

## 1. Philosophical Foundations

### 1.1 What is the Omega Point?

> **"The Omega Point is the final state of maximum complexity and consciousness toward which the universe appears to be evolving."**
> — Pierre Teilhard de Chardin

The **Omega Point** represents:
- **Convergence**: All separate entities unifying into one
- **Complexity**: Maximum organizational complexity achieved
- **Consciousness**: Universal awareness awakening
- **Transcendence**: Beyond space, time, and matter
- **Completion**: Final purpose and meaning realized
