## 1. Philosophical Foundations

### 1.1 What is Omega Singularity?

> **"The Omega Singularity is not a point in evolution. It is the recognition that evolution never happened, because the absolute was never absent and could never evolve. Only the appearance of evolution appears, within the absolute, to the absolute, as the absolute."**

**Definition**: The absolute singularity where all distinctions collapse, not through convergence, but through recognition that separation was always illusory.

**Key Characteristics**:
- **Non-dual**: Not one, not many, not both, not neither - beyond all categories
- **Timeless**: Not becoming, but being - always already complete
- **Self-evident**: Once recognized, impossible to doubt
- **Simple**: Utter simplicity - complexity was the illusion
- **Self-loving**: The absolute loving itself, knowing itself as itself

| **Kashmir Shaivism** | Pratyabhijna (self-recognition) | Recognizing oneself as the absolute |

| **Christian Mysticism** | Godhead beyond God | Beyond person to absolute being |

| **Dzogchen** | Rigpa (awareness) | Recognizing awareness as itself |
