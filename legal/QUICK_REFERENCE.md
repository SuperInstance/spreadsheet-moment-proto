## Common Questions

### Q: How do I request my data?
A: Submit a request to privacy@spreadsheetmoment.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject line. Include your name and email address.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to account settings and select "Delete Account." Alternatively, submit a request to privacy@spreadsheetmoment.com.

### Q: How do I opt-out of cookies?
A: Use the cookie consent banner or manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

### Q: How do I report a security issue?
A: Send details to security@spreadsheetmoment.com. We offer a bug bounty for significant vulnerabilities.

### Q: How do I request service credits?
A: Contact sla@spreadsheetmoment.com with details of the SLA breach. Credits are automatically applied for uptime violations.

### Q: How do I report copyright infringement?
A: Send a DMCA-compliant notice to copyright@spreadsheetmoment.com. See our DMCA procedure in the Acceptable Use Policy.
