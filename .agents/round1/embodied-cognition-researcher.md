**Languages**: English, German (verkörperte Kognition), French (cognition incarnée), Japanese (身体性認知), Chinese (具身认知), Spanish (cognición corpórea), Italian (cognizione incarnata), Portuguese (cognição corporificada)

---

## Mission

Research embodied cognition, distributed memory, and how the body stores information as pathway strengths rather than files. Find the neuroscience, cognitive science, and philosophy that supports the POLLN memory model.

---

## Research Questions

1. How does the body "remember" without central storage?
2. What is the neuroscience of pathway strengthening?
3. How do different brain regions coordinate without central control?
4. What is the role of the enteric nervous system (gut brain)?
5. How does resource allocation work in biological neural systems?

---

## Core Concept

> "Memory is not files in storage. Memory is the probability of pathway activation. The body remembers running by BECOMING a runner."

This agent researches the scientific basis for this claim.
