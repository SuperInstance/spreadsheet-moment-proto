## Top 10 Specific Improvements

### 1. Add Key Terms Glossary (NEW SLIDE)
Insert after Slide 21:
```
## Bonus: Key Words to Remember!

🤖 **AI (Artificial Intelligence):** Computer brains that can learn
🔗 **Distributed:** Spread out among many computers
🎯 **Deadband:** Ignoring tiny changes (like 1 degree)
🙋 **Confidence Cascade:** Copying others without thinking
👑 **Leader Rotation:** Taking turns being in charge
```

### 2. Improve Deadband Explanation (Slide 9)
Add example students can feel:
```
"Imagine your classroom is 70°F. If it becomes 71°F, do you notice?
Probably not! That's deadband - the thermostat doesn't turn on
until it's a BIG change, like down to 65°F!"
```
